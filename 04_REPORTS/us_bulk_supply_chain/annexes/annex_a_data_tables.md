## A.5 Barge Rate Benchmarks by Corridor — USDA GTR Segments

The USDA Grain Transportation Report (GTR) publishes weekly barge rate data for 7 corridor segments. While grain-focused, these benchmarks serve as the primary reference for all inland barge freight pricing. Rates shown represent the typical annual range (low/high) expressed as a percentage of the GTR tariff benchmark and in approximate dollars per ton.

| GTR Segment | Origin Region | Destination | River Miles | Tariff Rate ($/ton) | Typical Spot Range (% of tariff) | Typical Spot Range ($/ton) | Peak Season Premium |
|-------------|--------------|-------------|-------------|--------------------|---------------------------------|---------------------------|-------------------|
| 1 — Upper Mississippi (Minneapolis) | MN/WI | New Orleans | 1,850 | $30.74 | 175--475% | $22--35 | Sep--Nov: 250--475% |
| 2 — Upper Mississippi (Mid) | IA/IL | New Orleans | 1,200 | $21.15 | 150--400% | $16--28 | Sep--Nov: 200--400% |
| 3 — Lower Mississippi (St. Louis) | MO/IL | New Orleans | 180 | $5.89 | 200--550% | $6--12 | Sep--Nov: 300--550% |
| 4 — Illinois River | IL | New Orleans | 1,070 | $18.47 | 175--450% | $17--27 | Sep--Nov: 250--450% |
| 5 — Ohio River (Cincinnati) | OH/KY/IN | New Orleans | 1,530 | $23.82 | 150--375% | $18--28 | Oct--Dec: 200--375% |
| 6 — Ohio River (Upper - Pittsburgh) | PA/WV | New Orleans | 1,950 | $29.44 | 150--350% | $24--38 | Oct--Dec: 200--350% |
| 7 — Lower Ohio / Tennessee | KY/TN | New Orleans | 850 | $14.62 | 175--400% | $13--22 | Oct--Dec: 225--400% |

**Notes:**
- Tariff rates are historical benchmarks; actual spot rates are expressed as a percentage of tariff
- Rates are southbound (loaded); northbound (return/empty or backhaul) rates are typically 40--60% of southbound
- Low-water surcharges of $5--15/ton are common during drought conditions (reference: 2022 Mississippi low-water event)
- Harvest-season rates (September--December) typically 50--200% above off-season due to grain export demand

*Source: USDA Agricultural Marketing Service, Grain Transportation Report (GTR), weekly publications.*
