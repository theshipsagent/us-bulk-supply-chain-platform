## Key Findings

### Louisiana LNG Export Infrastructure:
The Louisiana Gulf Coast has become a major hub for LNG export with multiple operational and proposed feed pipelines including:
- Gulf Run (Operational 2023)
- TransCameron (Under Construction)
- NG3 Pipeline (Development)
- Texas to Louisiana Energy Pathway (Development)
- Hugh Brinson (Development)
- Port Arthur-Louisiana Connector (Development)

### Permian Basin Connectivity:
Extensive buildout of Permian to Gulf Coast infrastructure across all commodity types:
- **Natural Gas:** 6+ major pipelines (8+ Bcf/d capacity)
- **Crude Oil:** 10+ pipelines (3+ MMbbl/d capacity)
- **NGLs:** 5+ pipelines serving Gulf Coast fractionation

### Pipeline Reversals:
Multiple historic reversals to accommodate shale production growth:
- Capline (2020-2022) - Now flows south to Louisiana
- Trunkline (2017) - Natural gas to Louisiana
- Multiple Tennessee Gas/Transco expansions
