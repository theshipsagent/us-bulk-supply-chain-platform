## Usage Recommendations

**For AMAX Site Analysis:**
- Focus on Louisiana natural gas projects (Gator Express, LEG, NG3, TransCameron proximity)
- Reference Gulf South Pipeline and other intrastate systems for interconnection opportunities
- Use crude oil data (Harvest CAM, Bayou Bridge, Capline) for regional petroleum infrastructure context

**For Construction Materials Market (Case B):**
- LNG feed pipeline construction (LEG, Port Arthur connector, Rio Bravo, Whistler) requires concrete for compressor stations
- Focus on "Under Construction" and "Development" stage projects for 2024-2030 materials demand forecast

**For Pipeline Integration (Case A):**
- Transco, Texas Eastern, Tennessee Gas, Gulf South systems are potential interconnection targets
- Storage facility development would support LNG load balancing (multiple LNG feed pipelines in region)
