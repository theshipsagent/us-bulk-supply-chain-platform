# Florida Cement Market Analysis
## Executive Summary

**Prepared For:** SESCO Cement Corp
**Report Date:** December 16, 2025
**Focus:** Tampa Bay Region Market Entry

---

## Market Opportunity Overview

Florida represents the **second-largest cement consuming state** and **fourth-largest producing state** in the US. The Tampa Bay region receives **75% of Florida's cement imports** (6.8M MT annually), making it the strategic center for cement distribution in the state.

SESCO's new **Port Redwing terminal in Gibsonton** positions the company to capture significant market share with favorable sourcing from Egypt and Turkey (10% tariff) versus competitors facing 25-46% duties on Canadian, Mexican, and Vietnamese supply.

## Export Opportunity

**68.8% of US cement exports** go to Caribbean/Central America. Florida ports are ideally positioned for re-export.

### Priority Export Markets

| Market | Opportunity | Notes |
|--------|-------------|-------|
| **Bahamas** | High | 100% import-dependent, active buyer |
| **Trinidad & Tobago** | High | Zero duty (2025), government encouraging |
| **USVI** | High | US territory, 100% import-dependent |
| **Aruba/Curacao** | Medium | Tourism construction, established trade |

**Transit Advantage:** Tampa to Caribbean 5-8 days vs. 30+ days from Asia

## Strategic Recommendations

### 1. Product Strategy
- **Lead with white cement** - highest margins, least competition
- **Gray cement for volume** - competitive pricing, build relationships
- **Specialty products** - masonry cement, blended cement

### 2. Customer Priorities
1. **Stucco/EIFS manufacturers** - white cement, high volume, high margin
2. **Independent ready-mix** - gray cement, volume builder
3. **Tile/mortar producers** - white cement specialty

### 3. Geographic Focus
- **Phase 1:** Tampa Bay (1-hour radius)
- **Phase 2:** Central Florida (I-4 corridor)
- **Phase 3:** Caribbean export markets

### 4. Volume Targets

| Segment | Year 1 | Year 2 | Year 3 |
|---------|--------|--------|--------|
| Gray Cement | 25-50K MT | 75-100K MT | 150K+ MT |
| White Cement | 5-10K MT | 15-25K MT | 40-50K MT |
