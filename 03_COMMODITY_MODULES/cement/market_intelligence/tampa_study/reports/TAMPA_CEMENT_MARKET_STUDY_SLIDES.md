## SLIDE 37: RISK ASSESSMENT & MITIGATION

**Title:** Risk Assessment: Identified Risks & Mitigation Strategies

**Content:**

**TOP 5 RISKS & MITIGATION**

**1. COMPETITIVE PRICE WAR**
• **Risk:** Incumbents (Argos, Martin Marietta) aggressively cut prices
• **Probability:** Medium (40-50%) | **Impact:** High
• **Mitigation:**
  - White cement differentiation (less price-sensitive)
  - Service and technical support value proposition
  - Tariff cost advantage ($12-27/MT)
  - Target independents (less vulnerable)
  - Financial reserves for temporary margin compression

**2. PORT REDWING OPERATIONAL ISSUES**
• **Risk:** Equipment failures, permit issues, labor problems
• **Probability:** Medium (30-40% Year 1) | **Impact:** High
• **Mitigation:**
  - Equipment redundancy and maintenance
  - Backup discharge arrangements (other Tampa terminals)
  - Emergency response plan
  - Insurance coverage
  - Experienced terminal manager

**3. SUPPLIER DELIVERY FAILURES**
• **Risk:** Egyptian/Turkish supplier fails to deliver volume/quality
• **Probability:** Low-Medium (15-25%) | **Impact:** High
• **Mitigation:**
  - Multiple suppliers (Royal, Cleopatra, Nuh)
  - Long-term contracts with volume commitments
  - Inventory buffer (45-90 days)
  - Alternative source identification
  - Force majeure provisions in customer contracts

**4. MARKET DEMAND DOWNTURN**
• **Risk:** Florida construction activity declines (recession, rates)
• **Probability:** Low-Medium (20-30%) | **Impact:** High
• **Mitigation:**
  - Geographic diversification (Florida + export)
  - Product diversification (gray + white, multiple segments)
  - Variable cost structure (contract trucking)
  - Caribbean export optionality
  - Financial reserves

**5. TARIFF POLICY CHANGES**
• **Risk:** US government increases tariffs on Egypt/Turkey cement
• **Probability:** Low-Medium (15-25%) | **Impact:** High
• **Mitigation:**
  - Diversified sourcing (multiple countries)
  - Customer contract provisions (tariff pass-through)
  - Political/trade monitoring
  - Even with increase to 20-25%, still competitive vs. 46% (Vietnam)

**RISK MANAGEMENT APPROACH:**
✓ Monthly risk monitoring and review
✓ Contingency plans for top risks
✓ Insurance coverage (terminal, inventory, business interruption)
✓ Financial reserves for operational challenges
