# USGS Cement Statistics Summary
**Source:** USGS National Minerals Information Center (MCS 2025)
**Compiled:** December 16, 2025

---

## US Production (2024)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Portland/Blended Cement | 84 million tons (down 4% from 2023) |
| Masonry Cement | 2.2 million tons (down 4%) |
| Total Plants | 99 in 34 states + PR |
| Capacity Utilization | 72% (86 MT produced vs 120 MT capacity) |

### Top Producing States
1. Texas
2. Missouri
3. California
4. **Florida** (4th)

Top 4 states = 43% of US production

---

## US Consumption (2024)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| Total Shipments | 110 million tons |
| Market Value | $17 billion |
| YoY Change | Down 6% |

### Top Consuming States
1. Texas
2. **Florida** (2nd)
3. California

### Customer Breakdown
- Ready-mix producers: 70-75%
- Concrete product manufacturers: 12%
- Contractors: 8-10%
- Other: 5-10%

---

## Florida-Specific Data

### Production
- **Rank:** 4th largest cement-producing state
- Part of "Big 4" states (TX, MO, CA, FL) = 43% of US production

### Consumption
- **Rank:** 2nd largest cement-consuming state
- **Masonry Cement:** #1 state for consumption

### May 2025 Data
- **Clinker Production:** 4th largest (after CA, MO, TX)
- **Masonry Cement:** Florida, Texas, CA, NC, GA received 54.3% of US shipments

---

## Key Trends

### Short-Term (2024)
- Production: Down 4%
- Consumption: Down 6%
- Imports: Slight decrease

### Medium-Term (2014-2024)
- Average growth: 1.5% annually
- Shipments: 99.4 MT (2014) → 115.6 MT (2024)

### Industry Constraints
1. Closed/idle plant capacity
2. Plant modernization disruptions
3. Import price competition ($79/ton average)
4. Regional demand concentration
