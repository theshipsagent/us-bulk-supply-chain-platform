## Market Overview

### US White Cement Supply

White cement supply in the US is severely constrained:

| Source | Type | Capacity | Notes |
|--------|------|----------|-------|
| **Lehigh White Cement** | Domestic Producer | 140,000 MT/yr | Only US white cement plant (Texas) |
| **SESCO Cement** | Importer | Expanding | Egypt (Royal, Cleopatra), Turkey (Nuh) |
| **CIMSA Americas** | Importer | Limited | Turkish supply |
| **Other Importers** | Various | Fragmented | Limited scale operations |

**Key Insight:** US white cement market is essentially import-dependent due to single domestic producer

### Price Premium

| Product | Price Range | Premium vs Gray |
|---------|-------------|-----------------|
| Gray Cement | $79-95/ton | Baseline |
| White Cement | $180-280/ton | 2-3x premium |

**Margin Opportunity:** White cement offers significantly higher margins despite higher sourcing costs

## Florida Market Drivers

### 1. Hurricane Building Codes

Florida's stringent hurricane codes drive unique demand:

- **Stucco/EIFS exteriors** required for wind resistance
- **Impact-resistant construction** favors cement-based materials
- **Reconstruction cycles** create demand spikes post-hurricane

### 2. Climate Suitability

- **High humidity** favors cement-based stucco over alternatives
- **Heat reflection** - white cement reflects solar heat (energy efficiency)
- **Salt resistance** - coastal construction requires durable exteriors

### 3. Demographic Growth

- **Population growth:** Florida #3 state, Tampa Bay fastest-growing metro
- **Construction activity:** Residential, commercial, hospitality boom
- **Tourism infrastructure:** Resorts, hotels, attractions drive premium construction

- Multiple sources (Egypt + Turkey) - supply security
- Established import infrastructure (Houston experience)

## Export Opportunity (Caribbean White Cement)

### Market Context

Caribbean markets offer additional white cement opportunity:

- **Luxury resort construction:** 151 projects, 31,300 rooms (2024-2028)
- **Heat-reflective architecture:** Tropical climate drives white cement use
- **Premium positioning:** Quality and speed valued over price
- **Transit advantage:** Tampa 5-8 days vs. 30+ from Asia

### Target Export Markets

| Market | Opportunity | White Cement Application |
|--------|-------------|-------------------------|
| **Bahamas** | High | Resort construction, residential |
| **Turks & Caicos** | High | Luxury resort development |
| **Cayman Islands** | Medium | High-end construction |
| **USVI** | High | US territory, quality preference |
| **Aruba/Curacao** | Medium | Tourism infrastructure |

### Export Strategy

1. **Small lot capability:** Bagged white cement for smaller markets
2. **Container shipping:** Flexible volumes for various destinations
3. **Premium positioning:** Quality + fast delivery justifies premium
4. **Local partnerships:** Distributors in each market
