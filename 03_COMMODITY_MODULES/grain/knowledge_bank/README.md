## Key Reference Benchmarks

### Modal Shares (USDA 2014)
- **Domestic:** Truck 53%, Rail 28%, Barge 19%
- **Export:** Barge 55%, Rail 38%, Truck 7%
- **Gulf corn export specifically:** ~90% barge

### Transport Cost Crossovers
- Truck competitive: < 250 miles
- Rail competitive: 250-700 miles
- Barge competitive: > 700 miles (where waterway available)

### Rail Shuttle Economics (2009)
- Shuttle threshold: 75+ railcars
- Shuttle trains: 100-110 cars
- Grain = 8.4% of total U.S. rail revenue; 33% of all U.S. grain moves by rail

### PNW Wheat Export System (2021)
- PNW = 50% of U.S. wheat/flour exports
- Snake River carries 2-2.6 MMT/yr (13-16% of PNW exports)
- 242 grain elevators; 109 with rail access; 25 river docks; 5 shuttle facilities; 4 ocean ports

### Top Export Port Benchmarks (USDA AMS, 2011)
- **New Orleans Port Region:** 59.7 MMT ag exports; 33% of U.S. total; grain = 74% of exports
- **Kalama WA:** 9.5 MMT — largest single-facility PNW grain port
- **Gulf concentration:** Soybeans 63% of U.S. soy; Corn oil 85% of U.S. corn oil exports through Gulf
- New Orleans destinations: China 23%, Japan 19%, Mexico 8%, Egypt 6%

### Ocean Freight Rate Reference (January 2025)
- **U.S. Gulf → Japan:** ~$45/MT (Panamax, heavy grain)
- **PNW → Japan:** ~$26/MT (Panamax)
- **U.S. Gulf → China:** ~$44-62/MT (fixture range, 2024-2025)
- **Baltic Dry Index (BDI) context:** 726 in Jan 2025 = soft market; <1,000 indicates excess vessel supply

### Soybean Production Benchmarks (USDA NASS, ~2020)
- U.S. yield: 50.6 bu/acre (3.40 T/Ha) vs. Brazil 44.5, Argentina 40.0, Russia 21.9
- U.S. annual production: ~110 MMT; exports ~48 MMT
- SBM conversion: 1.5 MT soybeans → 1.0 MT soybean meal
- U.S. annual SBM production: ~51 MMT; exports ~14 MMT

### Global Grain Trade Context (USGC 2021)
- Global grain trade: ~576 MMT/yr (>20% of world consumption)
- Russia: world's largest wheat exporter (39.1 MMT in 2020/21)
- China: largest corn importer (29 MMT) and soybean importer (100 MMT) by 2021
- Brazil displaced U.S. as #1 soybean exporter
