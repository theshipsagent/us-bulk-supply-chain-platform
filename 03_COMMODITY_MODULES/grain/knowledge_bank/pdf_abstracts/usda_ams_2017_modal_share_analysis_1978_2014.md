## Summary

The authoritative USDA source for modal market share data for U.S. grain transportation.
Covers corn, wheat, soybeans, sorghum, and barley from 1978 through 2014. Uses STB Carload
Waybill Sample, USACE Waterborne Commerce, and FGIS inspection data to construct shares.

## Key Methodology

Modal shares calculated based on **last transport leg** within the U.S. before reaching:
- A domestic buyer (processor, feed mill, livestock operation), OR
- A U.S. seaport or land border crossing for export

**Data sources used:**
- Rail: STB Carload Waybill Sample (STCC codes by commodity)
- Barge: USACE Waterborne Commerce Statistics (commodity codes 14/15/17)
- Truck: Residual after subtracting rail and barge from total production/use

## Modal Share Trends (1978-2014)

### Overall Grain (All Commodities Combined)
| Year | Truck | Rail | Barge |
|---|---|---|---|
| 1978 | ~31% | ~48% | ~21% |
| 1990 | ~38% | ~43% | ~19% |
| 2000 | ~49% | ~31% | ~18% |
| 2014 | ~53% | ~28% | ~19% |

**Trend:** Truck share steadily increasing; rail declining; barge relatively stable

### Domestic Market vs. Export Market
**Domestic (2014):**
- Truck: ~53% | Rail: ~28% | Barge: ~19%

**Export (2014):**
- Truck: ~7% | Rail: ~38% | Barge: ~55%

## Commodity-Specific Notes (from TOC)

### Corn (Chapter: p.8)
- Primary export mode: Barge (UMR-IWW to Gulf)
- ~90% of Gulf-bound export corn moves by barge
- Domestic: Predominantly truck to ethanol plants and feed lots
- Rail growing for long-haul exports to PNW

### Wheat (Chapter: p.10)
- Export: Rail dominant (60% nationally per 2021 Jaller study)
- PNW exports: Barge 28%, Rail 60%, Truck remaining
- HRW from Southern Plains → Texas Gulf by rail or truck
- HRS from Northern Plains → PNW by rail or Duluth by rail

### Soybeans (Chapter: p.12)
- Export: Split between barge (Gulf) and rail (PNW)
- Domestic: Truck to crush plants (meal + oil)
- Brazilian competition intensifying on China market

### Sorghum (Chapter: p.14)
- Export: Primarily Texas Gulf by truck from Southern Plains
- China historically dominant buyer

### Barley (Chapter: p.16)
- Primarily malting barley domestic use; limited export volumes
- Pacific Northwest and Great Plains production

## Data Source Details

**STB Waybill Sample:** Confidential carload waybill sample; stratified sample of all carloads
for carriers with 4,500+ revenue carloads/year. Grain STCC codes used:
- 01112 = Corn
- 01131 = Wheat
- 01141 = Soybeans
- 01155 = Sorghum
- 01130 = Barley

**FIPS Rail Regions (Appendix B):** Specific FIPS county codes defined for each grain's
production region to determine which rail movements count as "export tonnage"
