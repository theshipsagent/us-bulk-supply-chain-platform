## Overview

The Russell Marine Group (RMG) presentation covers the U.S. grain trade from an industry practitioner perspective, focusing on five dimensions: Origination, Documentation, Domestic Transport, Trade Routes, and Export Trade. RMG specializes in bulk cargo services: supervision, cargo inspections, sampling, analysis, GMO testing, identity preserved (IP) handling, and freight forwarding.

## Soybean Production Benchmarks

### U.S. Annual Soybean Production
- **Production:** 110 million metric tons
- **Yield:** ~50 bu/acre
- **Export:** 48 million metric tons

### U.S. Annual Soybean Meal (SBM) Production
- **Produced:** 51 million metric tons
- **Export:** 14 million metric tons
- **Conversion ratio:** 1.5 MT soybeans produces 1.0 MT soybean meal

## Supply Chain Coverage

RMG organizes U.S. grain trade into five operational categories:
1. **Origination** — Farm procurement, country elevator interface
2. **Documentation** — Export certification, bill of lading, phytosanitary
3. **Domestic Transport** — Rail, barge, truck from interior to port
4. **Trade Routes** — Gulf vs. PNW routing decisions, vessel selection
5. **Export Trade** — Ocean booking, loading supervision, draft survey
