## What Was Accomplished

### Import Manifest ↔ MRTIS Vessel Matching (Complete)

Created `project_manifest/manifest_matcher.py` — a standalone post-pipeline script that enriches MRTIS voyage segments with Panjiva import manifest cargo details (commodity, tonnage, origin, shipper/consignee). Mirrors the architecture of `project_mrtis/fgis/grain_matcher.py` but for **imports** at **discharge terminals** instead of exports at grain elevators.

**Key design:**
- Dual-key matching: IMO (primary, exact) → vessel name key (fallback via `vessel_name_normalizer.py`)
- ±5 calendar day window between MRTIS `FirstTerminalArrivalTime` and manifest `Arrival Date`
- Filters manifest to `Port_Consolidated = "New Orleans"` (44,679 of 854,870 records)
- Filters MRTIS segments to 147 discharge-capable zones (Activity = "Discharge - Only" or "Load or Discharge")

## Known Observations

- **47.4% match rate** is below the 50-80% initial estimate. Many unmatched vessels are chemical tankers (Stolt, Bow) and container feeders (MSC, CMA CGM) — these may not have non-containerized import manifests in Panjiva for this corridor, or may trade under different operational patterns.
- **Vulcan St Rose** zone shows mixed cargoes (not just aggregates) — this is a geographically-defined zone that serves multiple operators, not a matcher error.
- The discharge zone count is 147 (vs 133 estimated in plan) because the Activity field has trailing spaces ("Load - Only " vs "Load - Only") — the strip() in the loader handles this correctly.
