

Based on the International Classification of Ships by Type (ICST-94) documents and their usage by organizations like the U.S. Army Corps of Engineers (USACE) and the Bureau of Transportation Statistics (BTS), here is the list of ICST ship codes.

The classification groups vessels into categories such as Tankers, Bulk Carriers, Container Ships, and Barges using a 3-digit code system.

* **113**: Oil Products Tanker
* **114**: Oil/Chemical Tanker
* **120**: Chemical Tanker
* **130**: Other Liquefied Gas Carrier
* **131**: LPG Carrier (Liquefied Petroleum Gas)
* **132**: LNG Carrier (Liquefied Natural Gas)
* **139**: Other Liquefied Gas Carrier

* **151**: Asphalt, Bitumen Tanker
* **152**: Molasses Tanker
