# Canadian National — Coal

**Source:** Web search results
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Overview

Coal represents 5% of CN's consolidated revenue (2024). CN moves both metallurgical coal (for steel production) and thermal coal (for power generation).

## Coal Types

**Metallurgical Coal:**
- High-quality coking coal
- Export to Asia (steel mills)
- British Columbia and Alberta sources

**Thermal Coal:**
- Power generation
- Domestic Canadian utilities
- Western Canada sources

## Source Regions

**British Columbia:**
- Northeast BC coal fields (metallurgical)
- Teck Resources mines

**Alberta:**
- Foothills coal (metallurgical and thermal)
- Historical thermal coal mines

## Destination Markets

**Export (Metallurgical):**
- Vancouver terminals → Asia (China, Japan, India, South Korea)
- Prince Rupert terminals → Asia

**Domestic (Thermal):**
- Canadian power plants
- U.S. utilities (limited, via interchange)

## Equipment

- Aluminum gondola cars
- Rotary-coupler cars
- Unit train operations (100-120 cars)

## Market Trends

- Declining thermal coal (power plant retirements)
- Metallurgical coal stable (steel production demand)
- Export markets volatile
