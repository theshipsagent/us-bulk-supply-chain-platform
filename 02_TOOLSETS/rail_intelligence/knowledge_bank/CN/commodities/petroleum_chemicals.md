# Canadian National — Petroleum & Chemicals

**Source:** Web search results
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Overview

Petroleum and chemicals represent 20% of CN's consolidated revenue (2024), making it one of the largest commodity segments.

## Commodities

**Petroleum Products:**
- Crude oil (Western Canada, Bakken)
- Refined products (gasoline, diesel, jet fuel)
- LPG (propane, butane)
- Asphalt
- Fuel oil

**Chemicals:**
- Petrochemicals
- Industrial chemicals
- Plastics and resins
- Caustic soda
- Acids and solvents

## Source Regions

**Western Canada:**
- Alberta oil sands
- Saskatchewan (crude oil, potash-related chemicals)
- British Columbia (limited production)

**U.S. Regions (via CN network):**
- North Dakota (Bakken crude)
- Gulf Coast chemical corridor (via interchange)
- Midwest refineries

## Destination Markets

- Refineries (Eastern Canada, U.S. Midwest, Gulf Coast)
- Chemical plants
- Distribution terminals
- Export terminals (limited)

## Equipment

- DOT-117 tank cars (crude oil)
- Pressure tank cars (LPG)
- General service tank cars (chemicals, refined products)
- Customer-owned/leased fleet
