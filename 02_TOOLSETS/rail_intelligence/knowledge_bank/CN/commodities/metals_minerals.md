# Canadian National — Metals & Minerals

**Source:** Web search results
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Overview

Metals and minerals represent 12% of CN's consolidated revenue (2024).

## Commodities

**Metals:**
- Iron ore
- Steel products (coils, plate, structural)
- Aluminum
- Copper
- Zinc
- Nickel
- Scrap metal

**Minerals:**
- Potash (see grain/fertilizers file)
- Coal (metallurgical and thermal)
- Sulphur
- Salt
- Aggregates
- Cement

## Source Regions

**Western Canada:**
- British Columbia (coal, copper, zinc)
- Alberta (coal)
- Saskatchewan (potash, uranium)

**Central Canada:**
- Ontario (nickel, copper, zinc)
- Quebec (iron ore, aluminum)

**Eastern Canada:**
- Labrador (iron ore via Quebec North Shore & Labrador)

## Destination Markets

- Steel mills (Canada and U.S.)
- Smelters and refiners
- Manufacturing facilities
- Export terminals (metallurgical coal to Asia)

## Equipment

- Gondolas (coal, ore, scrap)
- Coil cars (steel)
- Covered hoppers (potash, cement, sulphur)
- Flatcars (steel products)
