# Canadian National — Forest Products

**Source:** Web search results
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Overview

Forest products represent 11% of CN's consolidated revenue (2024). Canada is a major global exporter of forest products, with CN serving as a key transportation provider.

## Commodities

**Lumber:**
- Softwood lumber (spruce, pine, fir)
- Dimensional lumber
- Timbers
- Treated lumber

**Panel Products:**
- Plywood
- OSB (oriented strand board)
- MDF, particleboard

**Pulp & Paper:**
- Market pulp (bleached, unbleached)
- Kraft paper
- Newsprint
- Printing and writing papers
- Tissue products

**Wood Chips:**
- Pulp mill feedstock
- Export wood chips

## Source Regions

**British Columbia:**
- Interior BC (softwood lumber)
- Coastal BC (lumber, pulp)

**Alberta:**
- Northern Alberta (lumber, pulp)

**Ontario:**
- Northern Ontario (pulp and paper)

**Quebec:**
- Pulp and paper mills

**New Brunswick/Atlantic:**
- Lumber and pulp

## Destination Markets

**Domestic:**
- Eastern Canada distribution
- U.S. construction markets (via interchange)

**Export:**
- Asia (via Vancouver, Prince Rupert)
- U.S. (via border crossings)
- Europe (via Halifax)

## Equipment

- Centerbeam flatcars (lumber)
- Boxcars (paper, pulp)
- Covered hoppers (wood chips)
