## Overview

Union Pacific operates one of the most extensive intermodal networks in North America, with **32,000 miles of track** providing access to intermodal terminals in growing population centers across the western United States. UP's intermodal service connects domestic and international shipping lanes with comprehensive coverage of key markets.

## Commodities Shipped

### Container Types

- **Domestic containers:** 53-foot domestic containers
- **Refrigerated containers:** Temperature-controlled shipments
