# UP — Automotive

## Overview

Union Pacific is the **largest automotive carrier west of the Mississippi River**, providing comprehensive transportation services for finished vehicles and automotive parts. UP moves more than **five million vehicles annually** and serves all major automotive manufacturers in North America.

## Commodities Shipped

### Finished Vehicles
- Passenger cars
- Light trucks and SUVs
- Heavy-duty trucks
- Imported vehicles
- Domestic production

### Automotive Parts
- Components for assembly plants
- Aftermarket parts
- Automotive supplies

## Network and Facilities

### Assembly Plant Service
Union Pacific **directly serves five vehicle assembly plants** throughout its network, providing just-in-time delivery of components and expedited shipment of finished vehicles.

### Distribution Centers
Union Pacific operates or accesses **more than 40 vehicle distribution centers** strategically located across its service territory, serving all major automotive manufacturers.

### Geographic Coverage
- **West of Mississippi:** Dominant market position
- **West Coast ports:** Import/export gateway
- **Gulf Coast:** Import facilities for overseas production
- **Mexico gateways:** Six major border crossings
- **Canada connections:** Cross-border service

## Routes and Corridors

Key automotive corridors include:

### Import Flows
- **West Coast to interior:** California ports to distribution centers
- **Gulf of Mexico to interior:** Houston/Texas ports to Midwest and West

### Domestic Production
- **Assembly plants to distribution:** OEM facilities to dealer networks
- **Parts movements:** Component suppliers to assembly operations

### International Gateways
- **Six Mexico gateways:** Direct connection to Mexican automotive production
- **Canadian interchange:** Service to/from Canadian manufacturing
- **Port connections:** West Coast and Gulf Coast import/export facilities

## Equipment

### Multi-Level Railcars
Union Pacific operates the **largest multi-level railcar fleet in the industry:**
- **More than 14,500 multi-level railcars**
- Bi-level and tri-level autorack cars
- Enclosed autoracks for vehicle protection

### AutoFlex Convertible Railcar
In 2011, Union Pacific unveiled the **AutoFlex convertible rail car** production model, featuring advanced design for versatile vehicle loading configurations.

## Market Outlook

Automotive remains a stable, high-value commodity for Union Pacific:
- Strong OEM relationships
- Growing import volumes
- Mexican automotive production growth
- Parts supply chain integration
- Continued preference for rail over truck for long-distance vehicle movements

## Technology and Efficiency

- **Driver scanning technology:** Reduces truck processing time at terminals from several minutes to as little as 60 seconds
- **Limited truck idling:** State-of-the-art technology at terminals
- **Real-time tracking:** Vehicle location visibility for customers
- **Expedited service:** Priority handling for time-sensitive automotive shipments
