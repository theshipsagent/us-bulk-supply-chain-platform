# UP — Construction Products

**Source:** https://www.up.com/shipping/metals-minerals-forest
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

## Overview

Union Pacific provides comprehensive transportation services for construction materials, supporting both commercial and highway infrastructure projects across its 23-state network. Construction products span multiple commodity categories including metals, minerals, lumber, and specialty materials.

## Commodities Shipped

### Structural Materials
- Steel beams and structural steel
- Rebar
- Steel pipe
- Wire rod
- Sheet and plate steel

### Aggregates and Cement
- Rock and stone
- Cement
- Lime
- Slag
- Fly ash
- Sand

### Wood Products
- Dimensional lumber
- Engineered wood products
- OSB (Oriented Strand Board)
- Plywood
- Mass timber

### Other Construction Materials
- Wallboard/gypsum
- Roofing materials
- Building components

## Markets and Regions Served

Union Pacific connects construction material producers with building projects throughout its network:

- Major metropolitan areas across 23 western states
- Highway and infrastructure construction sites
- Commercial building projects
- Residential construction markets
- Distribution centers and building material dealers

## Routes and Corridors

Key construction product corridors:
- Pacific Northwest timber regions to Southwest markets
- Wyoming/Mountain West minerals to urban centers
- Steel from Midwest mills to West Coast markets
- Cross-border flows to/from Mexico construction markets
- Connections via Sunset Route and Overland Route

## Facilities and Terminals

Union Pacific serves:
- Building material distribution centers
- Lumber yards and reload facilities
- Cement terminals
- Steel service centers
- Aggregate distribution yards
- Transload facilities for truck delivery

## Equipment Types

Diverse equipment fleet for construction materials:
- **Center-beam flatcars:** Lumber and panels
- **Covered hoppers:** Cement and aggregates
- **Gondolas:** Rebar, scrap, coiled steel
- **Flatcars:** Structural steel, pipe, heavy equipment
- **Boxcars:** Packaged building materials

## Volume and Service Information

Construction products are a major growth driver for Union Pacific's industrial products segment. Volume is directly tied to:

- **Commercial construction activity:** Office buildings, retail, industrial facilities
- **Highway construction:** Infrastructure projects and highway improvements
- **Nonresidential construction:** Warehouses, schools, hospitals
- **Residential construction:** Housing markets

## Market Outlook (2025-2026)

**Strong growth expected:** Union Pacific anticipates continued robust demand from construction markets:

- Forecasted sales growth from nonresidential construction expected to fuel demand in 2025-2026
- Commercial and highway construction driving shipments of steel and construction products
- Infrastructure investment supporting aggregate and cement movements
- $3.3 billion UP infrastructure investment in 2026 enhancing service capacity

## Infrastructure and Investment

Union Pacific's 2026 capital program ($3.3 billion) includes:
- Track and bridge improvements
- Terminal expansion and modernization
- Equipment acquisition
- Technology enhancements

This investment supports increased construction product volumes and improves service reliability for time-sensitive construction deliveries.
