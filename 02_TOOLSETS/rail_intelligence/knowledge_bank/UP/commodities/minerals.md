## Overview

Union Pacific provides extensive transportation services for minerals and aggregate materials, supporting construction, industrial, and chemical industries across its network. Minerals are a core component of UP's industrial products segment.

## Commodities Shipped

### Construction Aggregates
- Rock
- Cement
- Lime
- Slag
- Fly ash
- Sand

### Industrial Minerals
- Soda ash (sodium carbonate)
- Specialty minerals
- Various ores

## Markets and Regions Served

Union Pacific's 23-state network connects mineral producers with customers throughout the western United States and beyond:

- **Wyoming:** Major soda ash production region
- **Mountain states:** Lime, cement, and aggregate production
- **California:** Major consumption markets for construction materials
- **Texas:** Cement plants and chemical facilities
- **Gulf Coast:** Export facilities and chemical plants

## Routes and Corridors

Key mineral corridors include:
- Wyoming soda ash to Gulf Coast ports and chemical plants
- Cement and aggregate from western quarries to metropolitan markets
- Sand movements for industrial and construction applications
- Cross-border movements to/from Mexico

## Facilities and Terminals

Union Pacific serves:
- Quarries and mining operations
- Cement manufacturing plants
- Chemical facilities (soda ash consumers)
- Aggregate distribution yards
- Construction material transload facilities
- Port export terminals

## Equipment Types

Specialized equipment for minerals:
- Covered hoppers for cement and soda ash
- Open-top hoppers for aggregates and sand
- Gondola cars for certain mineral products
- Unit trains for high-volume movements (especially soda ash)

## Volume and Service Information

Minerals and construction products are significant commodities driven by:
- Commercial construction activity
- Highway construction projects
- Industrial chemical demand (soda ash)
- Infrastructure investment

**Key Product: Soda Ash**
Union Pacific is the dominant carrier of soda ash from Wyoming's Green River Basin, the world's largest natural soda ash production region. Soda ash is used in glass manufacturing, chemicals, and detergents.

## Market Outlook (2026)

Commercial and highway construction drive shipments of construction products, consisting of rock, cement, lime, slag and fly ash. Strong demand expected to continue through 2025-2026, supported by infrastructure investment and nonresidential construction growth.

Union Pacific's $3.3 billion infrastructure investment in 2026 will enhance service reliability for bulk commodities including minerals.
