## Notes
Watco, operating as the Austin Western Railroad (AWRR), commenced operations on October 1, 2007. AWRR operates the line from Fairland, Texas to Giddings, Texas, in addition to a 6.4-mile branchline from Fairland to Marble Falls. Another 28 miles of track remain out of service between Fairland and Llano. The City of Austin owns the rail line from Giddings to Llano (190 miles total) and contracts AWRR to operate the freight railroad. Unique challenge: The City of Austin has commuter train services (Capital MetroRail) between Leander and Downtown Austin that utilize the same tracks as the freight railroad, requiring careful coordination.
