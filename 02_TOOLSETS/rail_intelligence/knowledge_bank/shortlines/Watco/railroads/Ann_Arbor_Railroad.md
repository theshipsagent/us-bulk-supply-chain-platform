## Notes
The Ann Arbor Railroad remained independent until being acquired by Watco in December 2012. Watco provides freight operations on the line owned by the State of Michigan. The railroad operates between Ottawa Yard in Toledo, Ohio and Ann Arbor, Michigan, utilizing former Ann Arbor Railroad trackage from the original incarnation. Following Watco's 2025 acquisition of Great Lakes Central Railroad, the AA benefits from streamlined routing efficiencies between the two railroads, allowing GLC to gain access to Watco's experienced marketing team. Watco invested approximately $3.7 million in the combined network.
