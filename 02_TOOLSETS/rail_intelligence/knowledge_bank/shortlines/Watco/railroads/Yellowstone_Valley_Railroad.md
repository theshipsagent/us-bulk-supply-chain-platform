## Notes
The Yellowstone Valley Railroad is a short line railroad that operates in North Dakota and Montana, owned by BNSF Railway and leased to Watco under an operating agreement. The railroad operates more than 170 miles of former Great Northern trackage in northeastern Montana, stretching into North Dakota, and began service in late 2005 handling traffic related to the natural gas industry including natural gas, crude oil, and frac sand. In 2004-2005, Watco started operations of the Yellowstone Valley Railroad in Montana. The YSVR is strategically important for serving the Bakken oil and gas region, providing critical rail transportation for energy commodities. This is one of two Watco railroads in Montana, along with the Mission Mountain Railroad (MMT). The railroad's focus on oil and gas industry support makes it unique among Watco's predominantly agricultural and forest products-focused operations.
