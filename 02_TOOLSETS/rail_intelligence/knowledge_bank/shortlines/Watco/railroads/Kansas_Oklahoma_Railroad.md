## Notes
The Kansas & Oklahoma Railroad (KO) is part of Watco's extensive operations in the Kansas-Oklahoma region. On the Kansas & Oklahoma Railroad, Watco primarily moves grain and grain products, and industrial products such as chemicals and liquid petroleum gases. The railroad serves the important agricultural regions of Kansas and Oklahoma, providing critical connections between local grain elevators and Class I carriers for movement to domestic and export markets. The KO is one of several Watco railroads operating in the region, including SKOL (South Kansas & Oklahoma Railroad), SLWC (Stillwater Central), and VESO (Verdigris Southern).
