## Notes
The Elwood Joliet & Southern Railroad (EJSR) started operations on October 28, 2020. EJSR leases and operates approximately 1.2 miles of rail line extending from a point in Crest Hill to Joliet, Illinois (Phoenix Line), leased from Wisconsin Central Ltd. The four customers on the line include a chemical blending company, a grain transloader, a scrap metal business, and a sodium carbonate provider. Despite its short length, the EJSR provides critical industrial switching services to diverse industries in the Chicago metropolitan area.
