## Notes
The Alabama Warrior Railway began operations on August 7, 2009. The railroad is owned and operated by Watco and operates 15 miles of railroad within the confines of Walter Industries in North Birmingham. The railroad primarily serves heavy industrial operations with coal, aggregates, pipe, scrap steel, and cement movements.
