## Notes
The Agawa Canyon Railroad was founded in 2022 when Watco purchased the former CN-owned Algoma Central Railway running 244.5 miles between Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario and Oba, Ontario. The purchase closed in 2022, with operations beginning February 1, 2022. This is Watco's first Canadian railroad operation. The ACR operates both freight and passenger services, with the Agawa Canyon Tour Train traveling 114 miles (183 km) through rugged Canadian wilderness, past dozens of crystal-clear lakes, over roaring rivers, and around more than 800 curves. Watco published its first Sustainability Report shortly after the ACR's startup.
