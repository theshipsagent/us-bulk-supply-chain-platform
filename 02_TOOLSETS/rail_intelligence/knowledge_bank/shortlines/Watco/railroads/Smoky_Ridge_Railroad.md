## Notes
Watco Holdings, Inc. filed a verified notice of exemption to continue in control of Smoky Ridge Railroad, L.L.C. (SMO). The Smoky Ridge Railroad in Tennessee joined Watco's short line network on February 2, 2026, making it Watco's 48th railroad. SMO seeks to acquire and operate approximately 7.07 miles of railroad track (the main line), together with approximately 6.63 miles of spur track, in Anderson and Roane Counties, Tennessee. The line extends from milepost 0, located at a switch on Norfolk Southern Railway's main line near Blair, TN, to milepost 7.07 near Oak Ridge, TN. The U.S. Army Corps of Engineers built the line and later conveyed it to the U.S. Department of Energy (USDOE). In 2003, USDOE granted Heritage Railroad Corporation (HRRC) an easement to provide railroad freight service, and in 2009, EnergySolutions, LLC acquired HRRC's easement. The transaction was consummated on January 22, 2026. SMO's projected annual revenues are not expected to exceed $5 million. This is Watco's most recent acquisition and represents unique operations serving federal government facilities related to nuclear research and operations.
