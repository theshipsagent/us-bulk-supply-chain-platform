## Notes
The Decatur & Eastern Illinois Railroad operates in Illinois as part of Watco's Midwest network. Watco operates the DREI and the Grand Elk Railroad (GDLK) in Indiana as connected operations. The railroad serves the agriculturally rich central Illinois region, with a focus on grain, ethanol, and agricultural processing industries. Decatur is a major agricultural processing center, making the DREI a critical link for agricultural commodity transportation in the region.
