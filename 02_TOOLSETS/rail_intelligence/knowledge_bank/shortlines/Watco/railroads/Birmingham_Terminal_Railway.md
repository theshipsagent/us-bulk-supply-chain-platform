## Notes
Birmingham Terminal Railway (reporting mark BHRR) is a subsidiary of Watco, operator of several short-line railroad companies. The BHRR operates on 76.1 miles of track, providing switching services in the Birmingham, Alabama area. Watco took over operations of the Birmingham Southern Railroad on February 1, 2012. The railroad serves as a critical terminal and switching operation, connecting multiple Class I railroads and providing essential industrial switching services in one of the South's major industrial centers.
