## Notes
The Surface Transportation Board granted authority for Watco Holdings, Inc. to acquire control of Great Lakes Central Railroad, Inc. (GLC), a Class III rail carrier operating in Michigan. Watco filed a petition on March 6, 2025, seeking an exemption to acquire 100% of GLC's common stock. The Board's decision was made on August 29, 2025, with the exemption becoming effective on September 28, 2025. Watco officially assumed operations on October 1, 2025. The GLC has the reporting mark GLC. It was originally the Tuscola and Saginaw Bay Railway (reporting mark TSBY), formed on August 26, 1977. When Federated Railways acquired TSBY in 2006, reporting marks changed from TSBY to GLC. Watco's acquisition creates streamlined routing efficiencies with the Ann Arbor Railroad and provides approximately $3.7 million in network investments. This is Watco's 47th short line railroad.
