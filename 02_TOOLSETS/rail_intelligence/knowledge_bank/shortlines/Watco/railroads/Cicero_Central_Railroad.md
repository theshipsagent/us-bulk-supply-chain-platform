## Notes
Cicero Central Railroad is an Illinois railroad operated by Watco in the Chicago metropolitan area. On this railroad, Watco primarily moves pitch and other chemicals used to create plastics. The CERR provides critical industrial switching services in the Cicero industrial corridor, specializing in chemical and plastics industry support. The railroad's location in the Chicago area provides excellent connectivity to multiple Class I carriers, making it a strategic operation for chemical distribution.
