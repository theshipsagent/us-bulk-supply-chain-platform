## Notes
Timber Rock Railroad operates 160 miles of track mostly in east Texas but also reaches DeRidder, Louisiana. The railroad serves the important forest products industry in the East Texas/Northwest Louisiana region, one of the nation's major timber-producing areas. The TIBR's name reflects its primary focus on timber and forest products transportation, though it also serves other industries in the region. The railroad provides critical connections between lumber mills, paper operations, and Class I carriers for movement to national and international markets.
