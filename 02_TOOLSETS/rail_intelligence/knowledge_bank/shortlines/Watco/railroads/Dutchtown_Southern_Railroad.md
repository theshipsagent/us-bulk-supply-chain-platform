## Notes
The Dutchtown Southern Railroad is a standard gauge switching railroad in Geismar, Louisiana controlled by Watco Holdings, Inc. that operates a 1.76-mile line leased from Illinois Central Railroad Company (part of Canadian National Railway). Watco launched the DUSR on January 16, 2021, making it the holding company's 44th U.S. short line and fifth railroad in Louisiana. The short line interchanges with CN, serves eight customers and handles chemicals and plastics. The DUSR is named after Geismar's Dutchtown High School. Despite its short length, the railroad provides critical switching services in Louisiana's important chemical corridor along the Mississippi River.
