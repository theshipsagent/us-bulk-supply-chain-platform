## Notes
The Autauga Northern Railroad is a short line operating between Maplesville, Alabama and a plant of the International Paper Company near Prattville, Alabama, covering 43.62 miles. The lease from Norfolk Southern was approved in February 2011, and operations began in April 2011 as part of Watco. Watco undertook a $2.39 million refurbishment of the railway to upgrade track to handle 25-mile-per-hour speeds. The railroad primarily serves the International Paper facility, providing dedicated service to the paper manufacturing industry.
