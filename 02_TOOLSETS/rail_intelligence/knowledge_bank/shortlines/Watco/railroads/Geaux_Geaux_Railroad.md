## Notes
Geaux Geaux Railroad maintains 26 miles of track north and west of Baton Rouge, Louisiana via Slaughter and Zachary. The railroad name uses the Louisiana French phrase "Geaux" (phonetic spelling of "go"), reflecting local culture and language. The GOGR is one of five Watco railroads operating in Louisiana, serving the important Baton Rouge industrial and agricultural region. The railroad provides connections between local industries and Class I carriers in the Baton Rouge area.
