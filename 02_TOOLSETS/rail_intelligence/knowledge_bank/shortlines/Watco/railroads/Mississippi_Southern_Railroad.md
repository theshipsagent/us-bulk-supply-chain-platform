## Notes
In 2005, Watco began operating the Mississippi Southern Railroad. The MSR serves Mississippi's forest products, paper, agricultural, and manufacturing industries, providing connections to Class I carriers. Mississippi is an important state for forest products and paper manufacturing, making the MSR a critical link in the supply chain. The railroad is part of Watco's southeastern network of short line operations.
