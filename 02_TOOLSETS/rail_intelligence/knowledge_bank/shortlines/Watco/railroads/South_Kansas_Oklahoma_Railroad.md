## Notes
South Kansas & Oklahoma Railroad (SKOL) is one of Watco's original and most established operations in the Kansas-Oklahoma region. The railroad serves the agriculturally rich areas of southern Kansas and Oklahoma, providing critical connections between local grain elevators, agricultural processors, and Class I carriers. The SKOL is part of a network of Watco railroads in the region including KO (Kansas & Oklahoma), KAW (Kaw River), SLWC (Stillwater Central), and VESO (Verdigris Southern). Watco is headquartered in Pittsburg, Kansas, and the SKOL represents the company's deep roots and commitment to serving the region's agricultural and industrial economy.
