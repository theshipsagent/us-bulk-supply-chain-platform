## Notes
Baton Rouge Southern Railroad (reporting mark BRS) is a Watco Companies property operating a very small segment of track north of Baton Rouge, about 1.5 miles. Despite its short length, the railroad provides critical switching and industrial service to chemical and manufacturing facilities in the Baton Rouge industrial corridor. The BRS is one of five Watco railroads operating in Louisiana, contributing to Watco's strong presence in the state's chemical and energy industries.
