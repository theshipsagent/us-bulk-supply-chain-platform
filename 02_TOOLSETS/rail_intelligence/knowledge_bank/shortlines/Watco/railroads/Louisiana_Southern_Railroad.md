## Notes
Louisiana Southern Railroad leases 157 miles of disconnected Kansas City Southern trackage in northern Louisiana. The railroad operates between Pineville and Gibsland as well as another segment between Shreveport and Springhill via Sibley. The LAS is one of five Watco railroads operating in Louisiana, contributing to Watco's significant presence in the state. The disconnected nature of the routes requires separate operational management but provides service to two distinct regions of northern Louisiana's forest products and manufacturing industries.
