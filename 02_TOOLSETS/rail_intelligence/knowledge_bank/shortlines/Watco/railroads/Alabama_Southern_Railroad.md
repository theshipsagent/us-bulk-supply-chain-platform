# Alabama Southern Railroad (ABS)
**Parent Company:** Watco Companies
**Location:** Alabama, Mississippi
**Route Miles:** 85
**Website:** https://www.watco.com/service/rail/alabama-southern-railroad-abs/
**Date Profiled:** 2026-02-23

---

## Class I Connections
- **Kansas City Southern:** Columbus, MS and Birmingham, AL
- **Norfolk Southern:** Birmingham, AL area

## Industries Served

- Steel manufacturing
- Paper mills
- Roofing product manufacturers
- Grain elevators
- Chemical plants

## Commodities
- Energy products
- Steel
- Paper
- Roofing materials
- Grain

## Special Equipment & Capabilities
- Interchange with KCS and NS
- Leased trackage operations
- Full service industrial switching

## Terminals & Facilities
- Operations between Columbus, MS and Birmingham, AL
- Leased from Kansas City Southern

## Notes
The Alabama Southern Railroad began service on November 20, 2005. The railroad operates about 85 miles of track between Columbus, Mississippi and Birmingham, Alabama under lease with Kansas City Southern. Primary focus on energy, steel, paper, roofing, grain, and chemical industries.
