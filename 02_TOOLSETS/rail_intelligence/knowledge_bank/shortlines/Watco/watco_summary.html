Key Insights

Largest Operation: Wisconsin & Southern Railroad (WSOR) with 837 route miles and connections to all six Class I railroads.

Most Recent Addition: Smoky Ridge Railroad (SMO) in Tennessee, acquired February 2, 2026 (48th railroad).

International Presence: Agawa Canyon Railroad in Ontario, Canada (acquired 2022) and Watco Australia operations.

Smallest Operations: Several railroads under 10 miles including Elwood Joliet & Southern (1.2 mi), Baton Rouge Southern (1.5 mi), and Dutchtown Southern (1.76 mi).

Strategic Focus Areas: Strong concentration in agricultural states (Kansas, Oklahoma, Idaho), Louisiana chemical corridor (5 railroads), and Alabama industrial operations (3 railroads).

Unique Operations: Agawa Canyon Railroad operates passenger excursion trains; Smoky Ridge serves DOE nuclear facilities; Jacksonville Port Terminal handles international cargo.

Major Investment: $3.7 million planned for Great Lakes Central Railroad network improvements following 2025 acquisition.
