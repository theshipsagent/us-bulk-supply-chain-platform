## Special Equipment & Capabilities
- Handles over 18,500 carloads annually
- Standard boxcars for lumber and steel
- Tank cars for carbon dioxide and chemicals

- Links New England's industrial heartland with national rail networks

## Terminals & Facilities
- 90-mile corridor between Massachusetts and Connecticut
- Multiple customer facilities and interchange points

## Notes
The Connecticut Southern Railroad has been a subsidiary of Genesee & Wyoming since 2012 when G&W acquired RailAmerica. CSO provides critical connectivity for New England industry, serving as a regional link between local customers and the broader Class I network. The railroad's diverse commodity base and strategic location make it an important component of New England's freight infrastructure.
