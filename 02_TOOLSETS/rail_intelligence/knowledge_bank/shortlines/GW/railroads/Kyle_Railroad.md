## Notes
The Kyle Railroad is one of Genesee & Wyoming's larger regional railroads, acquired through the RailAmerica purchase in 2012. The 550-mile KYLE provides essential rail service to rural agricultural communities in Kansas and eastern Colorado, serving as a vital link between local grain producers and the national rail network. Agricultural products, particularly wheat, soybeans, and milo maize, make up the majority of volume moved by the railroad. The KYLE plays a key role in moving goods the "first and last mile" and provides critical connections to Class I railroads BNSF and Union Pacific, helping businesses and communities maintain ties to the national supply chain.
