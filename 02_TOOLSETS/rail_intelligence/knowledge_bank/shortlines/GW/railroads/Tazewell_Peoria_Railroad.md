## Notes
The Tazewell & Peoria Railroad was formed by Genesee & Wyoming Inc. to lease the assets of the century-old Peoria and Pekin Union Railway (P&PU). The TZPR operates 142 miles of track in Peoria County and Tazewell County, Illinois, of which G&W owns 81 miles. The railroad switches an impressive 100,000 cars annually, making it one of the busier short lines in the G&W system. Strategic partnerships include interline agreements with four Class I railroads (UP, NS, CN, BNSF) plus regional carriers Iowa Interstate and Toledo, Peoria & Western. A key customer is Caterpillar's East Peoria manufacturing facility. Together with G&W's Illinois & Midland Railroad and other properties, TZPR is part of a 400-mile contiguous network spanning eastern Indiana to western Illinois with connections to six Class I railroads.
