## Notes
The Alabama & Gulf Coast Railway is a Class II regional railroad owned by Genesee & Wyoming, operating 292 miles from Pensacola, Florida export terminals north to Columbus, Mississippi. The AGR includes trackage rights along BNSF to Amory, Mississippi, and trackage rights along Norfolk Southern from Kimbrough, Alabama west and south to Mobile, Alabama, providing access to two major Gulf Coast ports. In a significant recognition of the railroad's operational excellence, BNSF Railway selected AGR as the premiere partner and first shortline to participate in BNSF's Shortline Select program, which focuses on operational process improvements and expedited customer responsiveness. The AGR hauls approximately 61,000 carloads annually with a diversified commodity base heavily focused on bulk materials including coal, steel, chemicals, and limestone.
