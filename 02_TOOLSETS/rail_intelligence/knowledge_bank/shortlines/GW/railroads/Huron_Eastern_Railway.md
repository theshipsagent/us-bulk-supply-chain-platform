## Terminals & Facilities
- Operations throughout The Thumb region of Michigan
- Flint/Tri-Cities area operations

- Lines north from Holland to Muskegon and Fremont

## Notes
The Huron & Eastern was organized in 1986 to operate and purchase lines in Michigan's Thumb region from CSX and other railroads. Genesee & Wyoming purchased the railroad from RailAmerica in 2012. The HESR is one of G&W's more extensive regional operations, providing crucial rail service to Michigan's agricultural region and connecting inland communities to the broader rail network. The railroad's diverse route structure includes both agricultural lines in The Thumb and industrial lines serving urban areas.
