# Heart of Georgia Railroad (HOG)
**Parent Company:** Genesee & Wyoming Inc.
**Location:** Georgia
**Route Miles:** 219
**Website:** https://www.gwrr.com/hog/
**Date Profiled:** 2026-02-23

---

## Class I Connections
- **CSX Transportation:** Cordele, GA
- **Norfolk Southern:** Americus, GA; Helena, GA

## Industries Served
- Agricultural facilities (grain elevators, feed mills)
- Fertilizer operations
- Lumber and forest product operations
- Feed production facilities

## Commodities
- Agricultural products
- Feed
- Fertilizer
- Lumber and forest products

## Special Equipment & Capabilities
- Transports approximately 10,000 annual carloads
- Strategic connections with multiple G&W sister railroads
- Leased track from Georgia Department of Transportation

## Terminals & Facilities
- Cordele, GA (CSX interchange)
- Americus, GA (Norfolk Southern interchange and connection to G&W's Georgia Southwestern Railroad)
- Helena, GA (Norfolk Southern interchange)
- Vidalia, GA (connection to G&W's Georgia Central Railway)

## Notes
Founded in 1999, HOG was acquired by Genesee & Wyoming in June 2017 through the purchase of Atlantic Western Transportation, Inc. The railroad operates on 219 miles of track leased from the Georgia Department of Transportation. Approximately 2,000 of its 10,000 annual carloads are interchanged with G&W's Georgia Central Railway, creating operational synergies within G&W's Georgia network. The railroad primarily serves the agricultural sector, with strong traffic in feed, fertilizer, and agricultural products.
