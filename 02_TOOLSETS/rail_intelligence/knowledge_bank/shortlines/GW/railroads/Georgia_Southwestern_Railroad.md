# Georgia Southwestern Railroad (GSWR)
**Parent Company:** Genesee & Wyoming Inc.
**Location:** Georgia / Alabama
**Route Miles:** 234 (212 as of 2024)
**Website:** https://www.gwrr.com/gswr/
**Date Profiled:** 2026-02-23

---

## Class I Connections
- **CSX Transportation:** Multiple locations
- **Norfolk Southern:** Multiple locations

## Industries Served
- Grain elevators and agricultural facilities
- Peanut processing operations
- Chemical plants
- Clay operations
- Plastics manufacturers
- Stone quarries and aggregate operations
- Forest product operations
- Bulk transfer facilities (Choice Terminal)

## Commodities
- Grain
- Peanuts
- Chemicals
- Clay
- Plastics
- Stone
- Wood and forest products

## Special Equipment & Capabilities
- Hauls approximately 13,000 carloads annually
- Choice Terminal bulk transfer facility for efficient commodity handling
- Transload services
- 286,000-pound capacity railcars

## Terminals & Facilities
- Operations across southwestern Georgia (197 miles) and southeastern Alabama (15 miles)
- Multiple interchange points with CSX and Norfolk Southern
- Choice Terminal bulk transfer facility (opened 2019)

## Notes
The GSWR was originally a RailTex property, became part of RailAmerica in 2000, went independent, and was finally acquired by G&W in December 2008. The 234-mile railroad provides access to the North American rail network via its Class I interchanges. The railroad serves a heavily agricultural region, with significant grain and peanut traffic. The addition of the Choice Terminal bulk transfer facility in 2019 enhanced the railroad's ability to handle bulk commodities efficiently and attract new business.
