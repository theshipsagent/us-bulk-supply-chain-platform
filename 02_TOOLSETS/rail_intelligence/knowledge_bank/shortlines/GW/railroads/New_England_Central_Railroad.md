## Notes
The NECR is a Class III short line railroad acquired by Genesee & Wyoming through the RailAmerica purchase in December 2012. The 345-mile railroad is one of G&W's premier New England operations, connecting with every major east-west transportation corridor and offering interchanges with four Class I railroads. The NECR's exceptionally diverse commodity base includes everything from traditional forest products and chemicals to finished vehicles and intermodal containers. The railroad's strategic north-south route through New England and its multiple Class I and regional connections make it a critical component of the region's freight infrastructure.
