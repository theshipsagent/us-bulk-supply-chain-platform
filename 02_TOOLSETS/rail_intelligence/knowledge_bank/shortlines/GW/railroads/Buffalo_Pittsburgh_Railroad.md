# Buffalo & Pittsburgh Railroad (BPRR)
**Parent Company:** Genesee & Wyoming Inc.
**Location:** New York / Pennsylvania
**Route Miles:** 659
**Website:** https://www.gwrr.com/bprr/
**Date Profiled:** 2026-02-23

- **Canadian Pacific Kansas City:** Buffalo, NY
- **Norfolk Southern Railway:** Buffalo, NY; Erie, PA; Driftwood, PA; Freeport, PA; Pittsburgh, PA

## Special Equipment & Capabilities
- One of G&W's larger regional railroads (659 miles)
- Multiple Class I interchange points

## Terminals & Facilities
- Main line runs from Buffalo, NY to Eidenau, PA (north of Pittsburgh)
- Connections to Pittsburgh city center via Allegheny Valley Railroad
- Multiple yards and terminals throughout Pennsylvania and New York

## Notes
Operations began in 1988 over mostly former Baltimore & Ohio Railroad lines (originally Buffalo, Rochester and Pittsburgh Railway). The BPRR is one of Genesee & Wyoming's larger regional operations, with an extensive 659-mile system spanning from Buffalo to the Pittsburgh area. The railroad serves a diverse customer base with multiple Class I connections, making it a strategic link in the Northeast rail network.
