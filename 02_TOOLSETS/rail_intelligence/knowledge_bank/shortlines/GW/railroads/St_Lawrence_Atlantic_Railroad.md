## Notes
The St. Lawrence and Atlantic Railroad operates a strategic cross-border route between Portland, Maine, and Montreal, Quebec, and is owned by Genesee & Wyoming. The railroad serves major communities including Portland and Lewiston in Maine; Berlin, New Hampshire; Island Pond, Vermont; and Sherbrooke and Montreal in Quebec. The SLR provides a critical link between New England industries and Canadian markets, as well as connecting the Port of Portland with inland destinations. The railroad's cross-border capabilities and diverse route make it an important component of G&W's Northeast/Canada operations. SLR is part of Genesee & Wyoming Canada (GWCI), which is headquartered in Montreal and operates nine short line railroads and three switching operations across four Canadian provinces and three U.S. states.
