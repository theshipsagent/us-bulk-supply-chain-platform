## Notes
The IORY is one of Genesee & Wyoming's largest and most diverse regional railroads, nearly approaching Class II regional status. G&W acquired it through the 2012 purchase of RailAmerica. The railroad features an exceptionally diversified traffic base spanning chemicals, metals, grain, ethanol, lumber, plastics, and other freight. A notable development is the growing "steel corridor" along IORY, with Delta, Ohio marking the fourth steel customer to expand along the railroad since 2015. The IORY's extensive network and multiple Class I connections make it a strategic asset in G&W's Midwest portfolio.
