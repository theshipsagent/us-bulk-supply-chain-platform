## Notes
Genesee & Wyoming won a lease in 1997 to operate Canadian Pacific's 180-mile line between Sudbury and Sault Ste. Marie, Ontario. The HCRY plays a critical role in supporting the Northern Ontario economy, with coil steel from Algoma Steel and freight from Domtar's Espanola paper mill accounting for 80% of freight traffic. The railway received significant government support in January 2023, with the governments of Canada and Ontario each contributing C$10.5 million (plus C$10.5 million from G&W) for a total C$31.5 million infrastructure rehabilitation project. This investment underscores the railway's strategic importance to Northern Ontario's industrial base and the need to maintain this vital transportation link for the region's steel and forest products industries.
