# Arkansas, Louisiana & Mississippi Railroad (ALM)
**Parent Company:** Genesee & Wyoming Inc.
**Location:** Arkansas / Louisiana
**Route Miles:** 117
**Website:** https://www.gwrr.com/alm/
**Date Profiled:** 2026-02-23

---

## Class I Connections
- **CPKC (Canadian Pacific Kansas City):** Monroe, LA
- **Union Pacific:** Fordyce, AR; Monroe, AR; Collinston, LA

## Industries Served
- Agricultural facilities
- Industrial customers across northern Louisiana and southern Arkansas

## Commodities
- Agricultural products
- General freight

## Special Equipment & Capabilities
- Railcar storage: 1,823 available spots
- Can handle cars with up to 286,000 pounds capacity
- Independent short line operation

## Terminals & Facilities
- Main operations in northern Louisiana and southern Arkansas
- Interchange facilities at Monroe (LA), Fordyce (AR), and Collinston (LA)

## Notes
Originally opened in 1908, the ALM has remained independent of larger carriers through several corporate reorganizations. Genesee & Wyoming Inc. purchased the ALM along with several other Georgia-Pacific railroads in January 2004. The railroad operates 69 miles in Arkansas and 48 miles in Louisiana. Despite being part of the G&W family, ALM has maintained its distinct identity and operational independence.
