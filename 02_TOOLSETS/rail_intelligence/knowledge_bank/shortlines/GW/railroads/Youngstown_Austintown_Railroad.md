## Notes
The Youngstown and Austintown Railroad operates very short lines just west of Youngstown, Ohio, and was acquired by Genesee & Wyoming Inc. in 2008 as part of the Ohio Central Railroad System acquisition. The railroad specifically services the steel industry in the Youngstown and Austintown area. Together with the Youngstown Belt Railroad, Warren & Trumbull Railroad, and Mahoning Valley Railway Company, these four railroads collectively operate over 20 miles in the Youngstown region, primarily serving steel industry customers. The YARR interconnects with the Youngstown Belt Railroad at Leadville, creating an integrated network serving the historic Youngstown steel district.
