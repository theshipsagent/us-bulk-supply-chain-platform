# Georgia Central Railway (GC)
**Parent Company:** Genesee & Wyoming Inc.
**Location:** Georgia
**Route Miles:** 211
**Website:** https://www.gwrr.com/gc/
**Date Profiled:** 2026-02-23

---

## Class I Connections
- **Norfolk Southern:** Macon, GA
- **CSX Transportation:** Savannah, GA

## Industries Served
- Agricultural operations (grain elevators, food processing)
- Lumber and forest product operations
- Chemical plants
- Cold storage facilities (Agile Cold Storage partnership)
- Plastics distribution centers (Plastic Express facility in Pooler, GA)
- Minerals and stone operations
- Pulp and paper mills

## Terminals & Facilities
- Macon, GA (Norfolk Southern interchange)
- Vidalia, GA (interchange with G&W's Heart of Georgia Railroad)
- Savannah, GA (CSX interchange and port access)
- Pooler, GA (Plastic Express distribution facility)

## Notes
Acquired by G&W in 2005, Georgia Central's 211-mile route from Macon to Savannah provides crucial access to the Port of Savannah, one of the busiest ports on the East Coast. The railroad's strategic position linking interior Georgia to the coast makes it valuable for import/export movements. GC has recently expanded its frozen food product transportation through partnerships with cold storage facilities, and serves major plastics distribution operations in the Savannah metro area.
