## Notes
The Dallas, Garland & Northeastern Railroad was started in the early 1990s and is now owned by Genesee & Wyoming. The DGNO operates well over 300 miles of trackage through lease and outright ownership, making it one of G&W's larger Texas operations. The railroad's area of operations includes both the Dallas metropolitan area and lines extending to the northeast. DGNO connects to three Class I railroads (BNSF, Union Pacific, and CPKC) as well as two G&W sister railroads (Texas Northeastern and Kiamichi). The railroad has an exceptionally diverse traffic base spanning lumber, paper, food products, automotive parts, military contracts, plastics, chemicals, aggregates, scrap metals, and agricultural products. In 2024, DGNO was awarded $16.75 million for infrastructure improvements in McKinney, demonstrating ongoing investment in the property.
