# Arizona & California Railroad (ARZC)
**Parent Company:** Genesee & Wyoming Inc.
**Location:** Arizona / California
**Route Miles:** 205
**Website:** https://www.gwrr.com/arzc/
**Date Profiled:** 2026-02-23

---

## Class I Connections
- **BNSF Railway:** Cadiz, CA; Matthie, AZ
- **Union Pacific:** Phoenix, AZ (via trackage rights)

## Industries Served
- Petroleum gas facilities
- Steel operations
- Lumber operations

## Commodities
- Petroleum gas
- Steel
- Lumber

## Special Equipment & Capabilities
- Hauls approximately 12,000 carloads annually
- BNSF named ARZC "Short Line of the Year" (2019)

## Terminals & Facilities
- Operates from Matthie, Arizona to Cadiz, California
- Interchanges at Cadiz (west) and Matthie (east)

## Notes
The ARZC is a subdivision of the former Atchison, Topeka and Santa Fe Railway. Genesee & Wyoming acquired the railroad through its purchase of RailAmerica in December 2012. The railroad's strong performance and customer service led to recognition from BNSF as their Short Line of the Year in 2019.
