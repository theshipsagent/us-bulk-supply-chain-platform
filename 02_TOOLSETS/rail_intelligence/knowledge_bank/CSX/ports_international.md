# CSX Transportation — Ports & International Connections

**Source:** General knowledge (CSX website access restricted)
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Overview

CSX provides access to more than 70 ocean, river, and lake ports across its network. The company serves major ports on the Atlantic Coast, Gulf Coast, Mississippi River, Great Lakes, and St. Lawrence Seaway.

## Atlantic Coast Ports

### Virginia Ports

**Port of Virginia (Norfolk/Newport News):**
- Major container terminal (Hampton Roads)
- Coal export terminals (Lamberts Point, Pier IX)
- Largest coal export port on East Coast
- Deep-water access
- CSX serves all terminals

**Key Commodities:**
- Coal (Appalachian exports)
- Containers (Asia/Europe trade)
- Breakbulk cargo
- Automobiles

**Port of Richmond:**
- River port on James River
- Paper products
- Forest products
- General cargo

## Export Commodities

### Coal
- Appalachian coal to Hampton Roads (Norfolk, Newport News)
- To Baltimore (Curtis Bay)
- To Mobile, AL
- Destinations: Europe, Asia, Latin America

### Forest Products
- Paper, pulp, lumber to Atlantic and Gulf ports
- Export to Europe, Latin America, Asia

### Agricultural Products
- Grain to Gulf ports (via interchange)
- Poultry to Caribbean markets
- Food products to international markets

### Steel & Metals
- Domestic steel to export terminals
- Via Atlantic and Gulf ports

---

## Import Commodities

### Automobiles
- From Europe (Germany, UK) to Baltimore, Jacksonville
- From Asia (Japan, Korea) to various Atlantic ports

### Containers
- Consumer goods from Asia
- Via Norfolk, Savannah, Charleston, Baltimore

### Steel
- Imported steel via Atlantic ports
- Distribution to domestic markets

### Potash
- Canadian potash via border crossings
- Distribution to agricultural markets

## Service Features

- **On-dock rail service** at major container ports
- **Unit train operations** for coal export
- **Automobile distribution centers** near RoRo terminals
- **Transload facilities** for container-to-rail conversion
- **Direct vessel loading** for coal exports

---

## Competitive Position

CSX emphasizes:
- Access to 70+ ports (ocean, river, lake)
- Atlantic Coast port dominance
- Coal export leadership (Hampton Roads)
- Automobile gateway (Jacksonville, Baltimore)
- Growing container traffic (Savannah, Norfolk)
