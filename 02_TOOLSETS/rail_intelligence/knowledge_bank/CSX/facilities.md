## TRANSFLO Terminal Network

### Overview
**TRANSFLO** is a CSX Corporation subsidiary and the leader in transloading bulk commodities between railcars and trucks. TRANSFLO has been in the business for more than 50 years.

### Network Statistics
- **40+ active terminals** throughout eastern U.S. and Canada
- Coverage across CSX's 21,000-mile network
- Specialized facilities for bulk commodity transfer
- Strategic locations in major markets

### Services Provided
- Bulk product transfer between rail and truck
- Product handling and storage
- Logistics management
- Warehousing services
- Handling of **500+ product types** including:
  - Chemicals
  - Plastics
  - Energy products
  - Building materials
  - Minerals and aggregates
  - Agricultural products
