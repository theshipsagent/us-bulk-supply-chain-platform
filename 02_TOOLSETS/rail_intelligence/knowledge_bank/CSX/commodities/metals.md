## Overview
CSX transports a diverse range of steel and non-ferrous products. The metals business at CSX is composed of seven key market segments and historically generates about 6% of the business handled by CSX.

## Primary Products Transported
- **Ferrous scrap** - accounts for over half of steel business
- **Sheet steel** - accounts for over half of steel business
- Semi-finished products
- Steel plate
- Pipe
- Non-ferrous metals
- Long products (rebar and beams)

## Market Segments
The metals business is divided into seven key market segments covering:
- Steel production and processing
- Scrap metal transportation
- Non-ferrous metal products
- Finished steel products

## Warehouse and Distribution Services
CSX's warehouse facilities provide a network of metals warehouses with heavy-lift capabilities offering an array of distribution options, including:
- Forward positioning of inventory
- Just-in-time truck delivery
- Strategic distribution centers

## Geographic Coverage
CSX operates approximately 21,000 route miles of track throughout the eastern United States and Canadian provinces of Ontario and Quebec.

## Service Capabilities
- Heavy-lift capabilities at specialized facilities
- Dedicated equipment for various metal product types
- Integration with warehouse and distribution networks
- Direct service to steel mills, processing facilities, and end users
