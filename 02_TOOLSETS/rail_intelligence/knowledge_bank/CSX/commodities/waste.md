## Overview
CSX provides rail transportation solutions for waste management, offering an efficient and environmentally responsible alternative to truck transportation for waste products.

## Service Philosophy
Rail transportation of waste products offers:
- Reduced highway congestion
- Lower carbon emissions compared to truck transport
- Scalable capacity for large-volume waste streams
- Long-haul efficiency

## Products and Materials Transported
Based on CSX's waste transportation portfolio:
- Municipal solid waste (MSW)
- Construction and demolition debris
- Industrial waste
- Recyclable materials
- Scrap materials

## Equipment Types
- Enclosed containers for waste containment
- Intermodal containers modified for waste
- Covered cars for odor and debris control
- Specialized equipment meeting environmental standards

## Environmental Benefits
- **Reduced truck traffic** on highways
- **Lower greenhouse gas emissions** per ton-mile
- **Fuel efficiency** compared to long-haul trucking
- Support for municipal waste management goals

## Network Coverage
CSX's 21,000-mile network provides:
- Service from urban waste collection points
- Transportation to landfills and processing facilities
- Long-haul waste transportation solutions
- Coverage across 23 eastern states
