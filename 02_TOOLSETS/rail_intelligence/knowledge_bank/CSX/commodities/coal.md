## Overview
The CSX network runs through the heart of the Appalachian and Illinois Basin coal fields and has direct access to producers of all coal qualities. CSX directly serves over 75 active coal load-outs in nine states.

## Coal Producing Regions Served
- **Appalachian Basin** - Primary coal-producing region in eastern United States
- **Illinois Basin** - Secondary coal-producing region in midwest
- Direct access to all coal quality types (thermal, metallurgical, specialty coals)

## Products Transported
- **Thermal coal** - for electric power generation
- **Metallurgical coal** - for steel production
- **Coke** - processed coal product
- **Iron ore** - related to steel production

## Service Options

### Unit Trains (Preferred Method)
- Unit trains consisting entirely of coal products provide the most efficient pipeline to move coal
- Shipments in dedicated trainload quantities
- Seamless movement from mine load-out facilities to destinations
- Most cost-effective configuration for coal transportation

### Less-Than-Trainload Service
- Single cars or blocks of cars readily managed
- Suitable for smaller volume shippers
- Flexible service for varying demand

## Equipment Fleet
CSX's car fleet readily supports a variety of loading and unloading systems for coal and coal-related products:
- Modern hopper cars
- Rotary dump capability
- Bottom-dump capability
- Equipment suitable for various unloading systems

## Export Facilities

### CSX-Owned Coal Export Piers
1. **Curtis Bay** - Baltimore, MD
2. **Toledo Dock** - Toledo, OH

Both facilities provide coal export capabilities for international markets.

## Network Coverage
- Over 75 active coal load-outs served
- 9 states with direct coal load-out service
- Through the heart of Appalachian coal fields
- Access to Illinois Basin coal producers
- Direct routes to power plants throughout eastern United States
- Export capability via owned piers

## Industry Position
Coal transportation has historically been a significant part of CSX's business, though volumes have declined with changes in the energy sector. CSX maintains expertise in efficient coal transportation and continues to serve active coal producers and consumers.
