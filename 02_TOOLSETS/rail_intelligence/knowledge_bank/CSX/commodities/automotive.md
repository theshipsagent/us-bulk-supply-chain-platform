## Overview
CSX's automotive unit is an integral part of the North American vehicle distribution network, shipping nearly one-third of all light vehicles produced. CSX handles over 5 million vehicles annually.

## Key Statistics
- **5+ million vehicles** transported annually
- Nearly **1/3 of all light vehicles produced** in North America
- Sole rail provider to receive GLOVIS' distinction as top-performing finished-vehicle transport provider in North America

## Services Offered

### Core Transportation Services
Together with affiliated companies, CSX offers a variety of automotive transportation and handling services for:
- Finished vehicles (cars, trucks, SUVs)
- Automotive parts and components
- OEM (Original Equipment Manufacturer) shipments

### TDSI Vehicle Handling
TDSI is a CSX Corporation subsidiary that offers additional vehicle-handling services through:
- Network of automobile distribution facilities
- Storage locations
- Facilities providing service to Eastern, Gulf and Southeastern ports
- Specialized vehicle processing and preparation

### Freight Damage Prevention
CSX's Freight Damage Prevention group assists with:
- Load planning
- Equipment selection
- Blocking and bracing
- Training of loading personnel
- Custom load patterns designed for individual products

## Equipment Types

### Autorack Rail Cars
- **Bi-level rail cars** - for standard vehicles
- **Tri-level rail cars** - for standard vehicles
- Designed to transport pick-up trucks, minivans, and sports utility vehicles
- Each rail car has maximum load capacity of **10 to 15 vehicles**

## Recognition and Awards
CSX is the sole rail provider to receive GLOVIS' (Hyundai GLOVIS) distinction as the top-performing finished-vehicle transport provider in North America, demonstrating the company's commitment to quality automotive transportation services.

## Technology and Innovation
CSX has deployed self-operating shuttle systems at automotive handling sites to improve efficiency and service reliability.

## Network Coverage
CSX's 21,000-mile network provides:
- Service to major automotive manufacturing plants
- Connections to Eastern, Gulf, and Southeastern ports
- Distribution network throughout the eastern United States
- Access to major population centers and dealership networks
