## Overview
CSX moves millions of tons of granite, limestone and other specialty aggregates each year. The company serves more than 300 warehouses located in and near every major market in the Eastern United States.

## Product Categories

### Aggregates and Other Specialty Stone
- Granite
- Limestone
- Specialty aggregates
- CSX serves 18 ballast stone quarries on its 20,000-mile network

### Cement and Related Products
- Portland cement
- Hydraulic cement
- Cement clinker
- Fly ash (beneficial reuse)
- Slag

### Lime Products
- Common lime
- Quick lime
- High-calcium lime
- Dolomitic lime
- Hydrated lime
