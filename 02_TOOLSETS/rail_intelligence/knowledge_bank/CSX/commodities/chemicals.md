## Overview
Rail is the most efficient and safest method for shipping chemicals throughout North America. The breadth of CSX's network provides safe service from major chemical producing regions to consumption markets in the East.

## Service Philosophy
CSX's chemicals team works hand-in-hand with customers to develop innovative transportation solutions and provide ongoing support throughout the shipping process.

## Products Transported

### Chemicals and Plastics
- Industrial chemicals
- Plastics and resins
- Petrochemicals
- Specialty chemicals

### Petroleum Products
- Crude oil products
- Refined petroleum products
- Lubricants
- Energy-related chemical products

## Safety and Compliance

### Hazardous Materials Handling
CSX maintains comprehensive safety protocols for hazardous materials transportation, ensuring compliance with all federal regulations and industry best practices.

## TRANSFLO Services for Chemicals
TRANSFLO, a subsidiary of CSX, provides:
- Bulk product transfer and handling
- Logistics management
- Handling of more than 500 product types, including chemicals, plastics, and energy products
- Comprehensive access to bulk rail-to-truck transloading sites in North America
- Economic and productivity benefits of bulk transloading
- Service to customers not directly served by rail

## Network Advantages
- **21,000 route miles** of track
- Service from major chemical producing regions (Gulf Coast, Ohio Valley)
- Direct access to consumption markets in the eastern United States
- Connections to over 70 ports for international shipments
- Integration with TRANSFLO transloading network

## Geographic Coverage
CSX operates in:
- Eastern United States
- Canadian provinces of Ontario and Quebec
- Major chemical corridors including Gulf Coast to Northeast routes
- Access to major petrochemical production centers

## Equipment and Capabilities
- Specialized tank car fleet for liquid chemicals
- Covered hopper cars for dry chemical products
- Compliance with latest safety standards
