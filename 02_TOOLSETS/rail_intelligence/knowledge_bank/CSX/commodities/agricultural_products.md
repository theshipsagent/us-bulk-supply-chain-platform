## Overview
CSX serves grain elevators, feed mills, grain-processing facilities, bakeries, ethanol plants, export locations and soft drink production facilities in a combined effort to provide safe, cost-effective and reliable service.

## Network Coverage
CSX serves over 300 agriculture locations across a network that encompasses:
- 21,000 miles of track
- 23 states
- Service to 2/3 of the U.S. population

## Products and Services

### Grain Transportation
- Grain elevators
- Export terminals
- Grain-processing facilities
- Feed mills
- Bakeries

### Specialty Agricultural Products
- Ethanol plant service
- Soft drink production facilities
- Agricultural inputs and outputs

## Grain Unit Trains
- **Unit trains** are a safe, efficient way to ship grain
- Longer trains increase capacity on the rail network, providing better service
- CSX maintains **certified 90-car facility locations** for grain operations
- Unit trains provide dedicated, seamless movement from origin to destination

## Alternative Services

### TRANSFLO for Non-Rail-Served Locations
For customers without direct rail access:
- Truckloads of product are consolidated and transferred onto rail cars
- At the destination, product is transferred from rail cars to trucks
- Enables delivery to multiple non-rail-served locations
- Combines rail efficiency with truck flexibility

## Commodities Commonly Carried

- Processed agricultural products
