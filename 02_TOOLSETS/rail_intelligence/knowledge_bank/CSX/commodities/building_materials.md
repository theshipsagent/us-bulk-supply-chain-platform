## Overview
CSX provides service to more than 30 lumber and 20 panel mills, 40 production facilities for construction materials, and distribution centers for major home improvement retailers.

## Products Handled
- Lumber
- Wallboard
- Bricks
- OSB (Oriented Strand Board)
- Plywood
- MDF (Medium-Density Fiberboard)

## Equipment Types
CSX has an extensive freight car fleet to fit building materials shipping requirements:
- **Center-beam flatcars** - for lumber and long products
- **Boxcars** - for enclosed protection of products

Products are transported efficiently and damage-free through proper equipment selection.

## Services Offered

### Reload Network
CSX's reload network enables customers to take advantage of rail savings one truckload at a time.

### TRANSFLO
TRANSFLO, a CSX Corporation subsidiary, provides bulk product transfer and handling along with logistics management.

### Load Engineering
CSX's Load Engineering and Design Services team works with customers to:
- Design custom load patterns for individual products
- Assist with load planning, blocking and bracing
- Equipment selection
- Training of loading personnel

### Industrial Development
CSX's industrial development team provides:
- Site selection
- Location analysis
- Engineering design
- Development of appropriate rail infrastructure on suitable properties

## Geographic Coverage
CSX serves 23 states in the East, Midwest and South; the District of Columbia and 2 Canadian provinces. The skilled professionals bring a proven track record to their clients in the building materials sector.
