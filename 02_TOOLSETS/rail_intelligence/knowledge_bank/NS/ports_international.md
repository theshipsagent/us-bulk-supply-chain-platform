# Norfolk Southern — Ports & International Connections

**Source:** Web search results
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Port Network Overview

Norfolk Southern serves:
- **24 seaports** (Atlantic and Gulf coasts)
- **10 river ports** (Mississippi River system)
- **9 lake ports** (Great Lakes)

The company connects to every major Atlantic Coast port, as well as key Gulf Coast and Great Lakes ports, serving vessels sailing to and from Europe, Asia, and South America.

## Atlantic Coast Ports

### Port of Virginia (Norfolk/Newport News)
- Container terminals (Hampton Roads)
- Norfolk International Terminals (NIT)
- Virginia International Gateway (VIG)
- **Lamberts Point Coal Terminal:** 48 million tons/year capacity, largest in Northern Hemisphere
- Coal export (Appalachian)
- Import/export containers

### Port of Baltimore
- Seagirt Marine Terminal (containers)
- Curtis Bay coal terminal
- Leading RoRo port (automobiles)
- Breakbulk cargo

### Port of Charleston, SC
- Container terminals
- Automobile import/export
- Breakbulk cargo

### Port of Savannah, GA
- Garden City Terminal (containers)
- Automobile handling
- Breakbulk cargo
- Major Asia trade gateway

### JAXPORT (Jacksonville, FL)
- Leading vehicle port (automobiles)
- Container terminals
- Breakbulk cargo

### Port of Wilmington, DE
- Via Conrail Shared Assets
- Automotive, containers

### Port of Philadelphia, PA
- Via Conrail Shared Assets
- Container terminals, breakbulk

### Port of New York/New Jersey
- Via Conrail Shared Assets
- Largest East Coast container port
- Croxton intermodal terminal

## Gulf Coast Ports

### Port of Mobile, AL

### Port of New Orleans, LA

## Mississippi River Ports

NS serves 10 river ports along the Mississippi River system:
- Memphis, TN (barge interchange)
- Various Louisiana locations
- Barge-to-rail transshipment

## International Trade Routes

### Europe
- Atlantic ports (Norfolk, Baltimore, Charleston, Savannah)
- Coal exports, containers, automotive

### Asia
- Atlantic ports (Norfolk, Savannah, Charleston, NY/NJ)
- Import containers, export coal/grain
- Growing East Coast port share

### South America
- Gulf and Atlantic ports
- Coal, steel, containers

### Caribbean & Latin America
- Gulf ports
- Food products, containers

## Major Export Commodities

- **Coal:** Appalachian metallurgical and steam coal (primarily Lamberts Point)
- **Containers:** Various manufactured goods
- **Grain:** Via Gulf ports (interchange traffic)
- **Forest products:** Paper, pulp, lumber
- **Steel:** Domestic production to export

## Major Import Commodities

- **Containers:** Consumer goods from Asia, Europe
- **Automobiles:** From Japan, Korea, Germany
- **Steel:** Via Atlantic ports
- **General cargo:** Various manufacturing inputs
