## Overview

Norfolk Southern moves lumber, woodchips, pulpboard, pulp, paper, and scrap, serving numerous paper mills while focusing on extending business into the Northeast and Midwest. The company provides comprehensive transportation solutions for the forest products and paper industries across its service territory.

## Forest Products

### Lumber & Wood Products

**Products:**
- Dimension lumber (framing lumber)
- Timbers
- Treated lumber
- Plywood
- OSB (oriented strand board)
- Particleboard
- MDF (medium-density fiberboard)

**Equipment:**
- **73-foot Center-Beam cars:** Primary equipment for lumber transportation
- **Boxcars:** Enclosed transport for lumber, plywood
- **Flatcars:** Heavy timbers, specialty dimensional products

### Wood Chips & Pulpwood

**Products:**
- Wood chips (for pulp production)
- Pulpwood
- Biomass fuel

**Uses:**
- Paper mill feedstock
- Pulp production
- Biomass energy generation

### Wood Scrap & Residuals

## Competitive Position

Norfolk Southern's forest products franchise:
- Direct access to major Southeastern timber regions
- Serves numerous paper mills
- Centerbeam fleet for lumber
- Atlantic port connections for pulp/paper export
- Extending business into Northeast and Midwest

## Market Trends

- Housing starts drive lumber demand
- Containerboard demand growth (e-commerce packaging)
- Newsprint declining (digital media transition)
- Paper mill consolidation
- Lumber mill capacity optimization
- Biomass energy growth (wood chips, pellets)
- Sustainable forestry practices increasing

## Environmental Considerations

- Sustainable forestry support
- Biomass fuel alternatives
- Recycled paper products
- Carbon footprint reduction vs. trucking
