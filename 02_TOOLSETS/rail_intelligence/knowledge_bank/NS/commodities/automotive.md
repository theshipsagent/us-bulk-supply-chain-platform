## Overview

Norfolk Southern is the largest originator of auto shipments in North America. The company offers the automotive industry reliable customer service and cost-saving initiatives through their modal and supply chain management solutions for delivering both automotive parts and distributing finished vehicles.

## Network Scale

- **26 auto assembly plants** served
- **35 auto distribution terminals** in 17 states
- **3 Just-In-Time (JIT) Rail Centers**
- Largest auto shipment originator in North America

## Service Categories

### Finished Vehicle Distribution

**Characteristics:**
- Model of innovation for vehicle distribution in North America
- Ability to mix different manufacturers' vehicles on a single railcar
- Reduces overall transit times for customers
- Bi-level and tri-level equipment provided

**Equipment:**
- **Bi-level autoracks:** For larger vehicles (trucks, SUVs, crossovers)
- **Tri-level autoracks:** For standard passenger cars
- Enclosed autoracks (weather protection)
- Damage prevention features

### Automotive Parts Shipments

**Service Offerings:**
- Door-to-door delivery service
- Truck-competitive rates
- Transportation options with streamlined shipments
- Between major U.S. markets and Mexico's industrial centers

**Modal Solutions for Auto Parts:**
- Traditional boxcar services
- JIT Rail Centers
- Triple Crown Services
- Thoroughbred Direct Intermodal Services

## Assembly Plants Served

Norfolk Southern serves 26 auto assembly plants across its network, including major OEM facilities in:
- **Ohio:** Multiple assembly plants
- **Kentucky:** Multiple assembly plants
- **Tennessee:** Multiple assembly plants
- **Georgia:** Assembly operations
- **Alabama:** Multiple assembly plants
- **South Carolina:** BMW (Spartanburg)
- **Mississippi:** Nissan, Toyota
- **Other states:** Various OEM locations

## Auto Distribution Terminals

35 auto distribution terminals across 17 states provide:
- Vehicle unloading and inspection
- Short-term storage
- Damage repair facilities
- Accessory installation
- Final delivery staging

## Just-In-Time (JIT) Rail Centers

Three JIT Rail Centers provide:
- Time-sensitive automotive parts delivery
- Sequenced parts delivery to assembly lines
- Supply chain management
- Inventory reduction for manufacturers

## Routes

### Vehicle Distribution Routes
- Assembly plants → Regional distribution centers
- Port imports (Baltimore, Jacksonville) → Distribution centers
- Distribution centers → Dealer delivery points

### Parts Distribution Routes
- Mexico suppliers → U.S. assembly plants (via interchange)
- Domestic parts suppliers → Assembly plants
- Distribution centers → Just-in-time delivery points

## Mexico Connections

Norfolk Southern provides connectivity to Mexico's industrial centers for:
- Parts shipments from Mexican suppliers to U.S. assembly plants
- Finished vehicle imports from Mexican assembly plants
- Via interchange with Union Pacific and CPKC

## Competitive Position

Norfolk Southern's automotive advantages:
- Largest auto shipment originator in North America
- Direct access to major Southeast assembly plants
- 35 distribution terminals across service territory
- JIT Rail Centers for time-sensitive parts
- Multiple modal solutions (boxcar, intermodal, specialized)
- Atlantic Coast port connections (vehicle imports)

## Port Connections for Vehicle Imports

- **Port of Baltimore:** Leading RoRo port (Europe, Asia imports)
- **JAXPORT (Jacksonville):** Leading vehicle port
- **Port of Charleston:** Vehicle imports
- **Port of Savannah:** Vehicle imports

## Market Trends

- Auto production recovering from supply chain disruptions
- Electric vehicle production growth (new assembly plants)
- Nearshoring from Mexico (parts supply)
- Shift to larger vehicles (SUVs, trucks) affecting autorack loading
- JIT delivery requirements increasing
- Parts distribution complexity growing
