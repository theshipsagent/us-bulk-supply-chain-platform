## Overview

Norfolk Southern is a transportation leader in the steel industry and construction materials. The company serves over 50 steel processors, 150 scrap metal locations, 67 quarries, 30 aggregate distribution yards, and 15 cement facilities. Their metals and construction market comprises steel, aluminum products, machinery, scrap metals, cement, aggregates, sand, and minerals.
