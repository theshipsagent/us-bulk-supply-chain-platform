## Overview

Norfolk Southern helps customers safely ship organic chemicals, inorganic chemicals, waste, sand, NGLs, crude oil, petroleum, and plastics. The company safely ships chemicals and wastes, including petroleum products, plastics, industrial chemicals, chlorine, sulfur and related chemicals, and other non-hazardous waste.

## Equipment

### Tank Cars

**Regulated (Hazmat) Tank Cars:**
- DOT-111 compliant (legacy fleet)
- DOT-117 (crude oil, newer standards)
- Pressure tank cars (chlorine, ammonia, LPG)
- Food-grade tank cars (segregated fleet)
- Coil cars (certain chemicals)

**Customer Responsibility:**
- Tank cars owned or leased by customers
- Customers responsible for car maintenance
- Proper securing for transit
- Regulatory compliance

### Covered Hoppers

## Safety Programs

### Thoroughbred Chemical Safety Awards

**Program Highlights:**
- Annual recognition for customers handling large quantities of chemicals without incident
- 2023: 47 customers earned the award
- 12 plants safely transported 100% of shipments without incident
- Corporate manufacturers recognized for safety performance

### Safety Standards & Compliance

- Voluntary participant in American Chemistry Council's **Responsible Care® Partner Program** since 1996
- Observes strict standards to identify, reduce, and manage safety risks in chemical transport
- **Operation Clean Sweep Pledge:** Eliminate plastic pollution, zero loss of plastic resin into environment
- Hazmat training and emergency response programs
- First responder training initiatives

## Regulatory Compliance

Norfolk Southern ensures compliance with:
- DOT hazardous materials regulations (49 CFR)
- AAR tank car standards
- EPA environmental regulations
- Emergency response requirements (HAZMAT)
- Commodity-specific regulations (chlorine, ammonia, etc.)

## Service Types

- Unit trains for high-volume chemical producers
- Manifest freight for smaller chemical shipments
- Dedicated train service for certain customers
- Transload services (limited for chemicals due to safety)
