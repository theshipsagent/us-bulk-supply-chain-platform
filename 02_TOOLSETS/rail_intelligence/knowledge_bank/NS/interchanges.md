## Class I Railroad Interchanges

### Chicago, IL (All Class I Carriers)
**Partners:** BNSF, Union Pacific, CSX, Canadian National, Canadian Pacific Kansas City
**Facilities:** Multiple interchange points, Belt Railway of Chicago
**Commodities:** All major categories

### Kansas City, MO/KS
**Partners:** BNSF, Union Pacific, CPKC
**Commodities:** Intermodal, grain, automotive, chemicals

### Memphis, TN
**Partners:** BNSF, Union Pacific, CSX, Canadian National, CPKC
**Key Features:** Mississippi River crossing, gateway to West and Southwest
**Commodities:** All categories, heavy intermodal exchange

### Meridian, MS (Meridian Speedway)
**Partner:** CPKC (joint operation)
**Key Features:** 320-mile joint corridor to Shreveport, LA
**Commodities:** Intermodal (primary), automotive, general freight

### New Orleans, LA
**Partners:** BNSF, Union Pacific, CSX, Canadian National
**Key Features:** Gulf Coast port access
**Commodities:** Containers, grain, steel, chemicals

### St. Louis, MO
**Partners:** BNSF, Union Pacific, CSX
**Commodities:** Coal, grain, general freight

### Louisville, KY
**Partners:** CSX, regional carriers
**Key Features:** Recently expanded intermodal terminal

### Shreveport, LA
**Partner:** Union Pacific, CPKC (via Meridian Speedway)

## Conrail Shared Assets

**Partnership:** Joint operation with CSX in three Shared Assets Areas

**North Jersey:**
- Access to Port of New York/New Jersey
- Croxton intermodal terminal

**South Jersey/Philadelphia:**
- Port of Philadelphia
- Chemical corridor

**Detroit:**
- Automotive corridor
- Great Lakes access

## Shortline & Regional Railroad Connections

Extensive network of shortline connections providing first-mile/last-mile service across all 22 states.

## Port Gateway Interchanges

- All Atlantic Coast ports (24 seaports)
- Gulf Coast ports (Mobile, New Orleans, Pensacola)
- Great Lakes ports (9 lake ports)
