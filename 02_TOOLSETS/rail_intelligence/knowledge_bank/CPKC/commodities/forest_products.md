# CPKC — Forest Products

**Source:** Web search results
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

## Overview

CPKC transports forest products from Canadian sources to North American markets.

## Commodities

**Lumber:**
- Softwood lumber (spruce, pine, fir)
- Dimensional lumber
- Treated lumber

**Panel Products:**
- Plywood
- OSB
- Particleboard

**Pulp & Paper:**
- Market pulp
- Kraft paper
- Containerboard
- Newsprint

**Wood Chips:**
- Pulp mill feedstock

## Source Regions

**Canada:**
- British Columbia (primary source)
- Alberta
- Quebec

## Destination Markets

- U.S. construction markets (via single-line to U.S.)
- Mexico (via single-line service)
- Export via Vancouver

## Equipment

- Centerbeam flatcars (lumber)
- Boxcars (paper, pulp)
