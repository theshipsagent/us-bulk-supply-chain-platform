## Overview

CPKC transports metals and minerals across its tri-national network.

- Aluminum
- Copper
- Zinc
- Scrap metal

**Minerals:**

- Coal
- Aggregates
- Cement

**Canada:**
- British Columbia (coal, copper, zinc)
- Saskatchewan (potash)
- Ontario (metals)
- Quebec (aluminum)

**United States:**
- Midwest (steel, scrap)

**Mexico:**
- Mining regions (various metals)

- Manufacturing facilities
- Construction markets
- Export terminals

- Covered hoppers (potash, cement, sulfur)
