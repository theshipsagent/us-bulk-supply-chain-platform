## Overview

CPKC transports chemicals and plastics as major commodity categories across its Canada-U.S.-Mexico network.

## Commodities

**Chemicals:**
- Industrial chemicals
- Petrochemicals
- Agricultural chemicals
- Caustic soda
- Acids

**Plastics:**
- Plastic resins (PE, PP, PVC)
- Polymer feedstocks
- Finished plastic products

## Source Regions

**Canada:**
- Alberta (petrochemicals)
- Ontario (chemical production)

**United States:**
- Gulf Coast (via connections)
- Midwest chemical plants

**Mexico:**
- Monterrey industrial area
- Gulf Coast Mexico

## Destination Markets

- Manufacturing facilities
- Plastics processors
- Agricultural distributors
- Export (Mexico, international)

## Equipment

- Tank cars (liquid chemicals)
- Covered hoppers (plastic resins)
- Customer-owned/leased fleet
