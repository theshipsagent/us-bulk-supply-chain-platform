## Overview

Automotive products are a major CPKC commodity, particularly with Mexico manufacturing connections.

## Commodities

**Finished Vehicles:**
- Passenger cars
- Light trucks, SUVs
- Commercial vehicles

**Automotive Parts:**
- Engines, transmissions
- Stampings, assemblies
- JIT parts delivery

## Source Regions

**Mexico:**
- Monterrey region (multiple OEM plants)
- Aguascalientes (Nissan, others)
- San Luis Potosí (BMW, others)
- Querétaro

**Canada:**
- Ontario (Toronto area)

**United States:**
- Missouri, Kansas (limited assembly)

## Destination Markets

- U.S. markets (single-line Mexico to U.S.)
- Canadian markets
- Distribution centers
- Dealer delivery points

## CPKC Advantage

**Single-Line Mexico-U.S. Service:**
- No interchange delay
- Faster transit times
- Seamless cross-border movements
- Automotive assembly plants directly served in Mexico

## Equipment

- Multilevel autoracks (bi-level, tri-level)
- Boxcars (automotive parts)
- Intermodal containers (parts)
