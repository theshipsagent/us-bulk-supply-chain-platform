# CPKC — Grain

**Source:** Web search results
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Overview

Grain is a major CPKC commodity. In the 2024-2025 crop year (August 2024-July 2025), CN and CPKC moved 58.6 million metric tonnes of Canadian grain and grain products – 22% higher than the previous 3-year average.

## Commodities

- Wheat (Canada, U.S. Great Plains, Mexico)
- Corn (U.S. Midwest, Mexico)
- Soybeans (U.S. Midwest)
- Canola (Canadian Prairies)
- Barley, oats, other grains

## Source Regions

**Canadian Prairies:**
- Saskatchewan
- Alberta
- Manitoba

**U.S. Great Plains & Midwest:**
- Kansas (major wheat state)
- Nebraska
- Minnesota
- Iowa
- Illinois (via Chicago)

**Mexico:**
- Domestic grain movements

## Destination Markets

**Canadian Export:**
- Vancouver, BC (Pacific markets)
- Thunder Bay, ON (Great Lakes)

**U.S. Export:**
- Gulf Coast via Kansas City connection
- Pacific Northwest (via interchange)

**Mexico:**
- Import grain to Mexico for domestic consumption
- Feed for livestock, poultry industries

## Service Characteristics

- Unit train service
- Shuttle trains to export terminals
- Covered hopper fleet
