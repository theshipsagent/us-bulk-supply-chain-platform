## Overview

CPKC hauls energy products including crude oil and frac sand as major commodities.

## Commodities

**Crude Oil:**
- Bakken crude (North Dakota, Montana)
- Canadian oil sands crude (Alberta)
- Permian crude (limited)

**Frac Sand:**
- Wisconsin sand (high-quality proppant)
- To oil & gas production regions

**Coal:**
- Powder River Basin (via interchange)
- Canadian coal (British Columbia)
- Domestic and export markets

**Other Energy:**
- LPG (propane, butane)
- Refined products (limited)

## Source Regions

**Crude Oil:**
- Alberta oil sands
- Bakken Formation (North Dakota/Montana via connections)

**Frac Sand:**
- Wisconsin (primary source)

**Coal:**
- British Columbia (export coal)

## Destination Markets

- Refineries (U.S. Midwest, Gulf Coast)
- Oil & gas fields (frac sand delivery)
- Export terminals (coal)

## Equipment

- DOT-117 tank cars (crude oil)
- Covered hoppers (frac sand)
- Gondolas (coal)
