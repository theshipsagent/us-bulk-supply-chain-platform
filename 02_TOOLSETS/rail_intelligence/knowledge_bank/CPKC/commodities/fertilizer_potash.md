## Overview

Fertilizer and potash are major CPKC commodities. Saskatchewan is the world's largest potash producer, and CPKC provides rail service from mines to export terminals.

## Commodities

**Potash:**
- Potassium chloride (muriate of potash)
- Potassium sulfate
- World's largest reserves in Saskatchewan

**Other Fertilizers:**
- Nitrogen fertilizers
- Phosphate
- Blended fertilizers

## Source Regions

**Potash:**
- Saskatchewan (world's largest potash mining region)
- Multiple mines on CPKC lines

**Other Fertilizers:**
- Alberta (nitrogen production)
- Import terminals (phosphate)

## Destination Markets

**Export (Potash):**
- Vancouver, BC → Asia (China, India, Southeast Asia)
- Thunder Bay, ON → Great Lakes/Atlantic
- Pacific Northwest → Global markets

**Domestic:**
- U.S. agricultural regions (via Kansas City gateway)
- Mexico agricultural areas
- Canadian Prairies

## Service Characteristics

- Unit train service (potash)
- Covered hopper fleet
- High-volume export movements
- Year-round service
