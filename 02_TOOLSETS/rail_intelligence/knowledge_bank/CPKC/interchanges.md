## Unique Advantage: No Interchange Required

CPKC's single-line Canada-U.S.-Mexico network eliminates interchange delays for traffic moving between the three countries. Shipments from Vancouver to Mexico City, or Toronto to Monterrey, move on CPKC the entire way without interchange to another carrier.

## International Border Crossings

**U.S.-Canada:**
- Portal, ND / Estevan, SK
- Emerson, MB / Pembina, ND
- Multiple additional crossings

**U.S.-Mexico:**
- Laredo, TX / Nuevo Laredo, Mexico (primary gateway)
- Eagle Pass, TX / Piedras Negras, Mexico

## Shortline & Regional Railroad Connections

Extensive connections to shortline and regional railroads across Canada, U.S., and Mexico for first-mile/last-mile service.
