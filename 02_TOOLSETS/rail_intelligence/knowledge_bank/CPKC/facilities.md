## Special Facilities

### Kansas City Americold (Under Construction, Opens 2026)
- Refrigerated warehouse
- Beef and pork export to Mexico
- Adjacent to Knoche Yard

### Lázaro Cárdenas Automated Terminal
- Currently at 50% capacity
- Expanding service
- Alternative to LA/Long Beach for Texas-bound imports
- 10,000-foot stack train capability (future)

## Mexico Infrastructure Investments

CPKC has invested over $75 million in Mexico for:
- New sidings
- Double-track sections
- Bypass tracks
- Yard leads
- Faster turnouts
