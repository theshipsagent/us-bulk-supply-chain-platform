**Source:** Web search results
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

## Company Overview

**Headquarters:** Calgary, Alberta, Canada
**Route Miles:** Approximately 20,000 miles (32,000 km)
**Service Territory:** Canada, United States, Mexico
**Stock Symbol:** TSX: CP, NYSE: CP
**Formed:** April 14, 2023 (CP merger with Kansas City Southern)

## Unique Value Proposition

**CPKC is the only single-line rail network connecting Canada, the United States, and Mexico.**

This is the first and only single-line transnational railroad linking all three North American countries, created from the April 2023 merger of Canadian Pacific Railway with Kansas City Southern.

## Mexico Operations

- Over 3,000 miles of rail in Mexico
- $75 million+ invested in new sidings, double-tracking, bypass tracks, yard leads, faster turnouts
- New single-linehaul services from Canada/Upper Midwest through Texas/Gulf to Mexico
- Cross-border and intra-Mexico freight
- Operating concessions in Mexico
