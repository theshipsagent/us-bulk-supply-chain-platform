## Overview

CPKC operates approximately 20,000 miles across Canada, United States, and Mexico. The network's key corridors connect Vancouver to Chicago, Chicago to Kansas City, and Kansas City to Mexico City and Lázaro Cárdenas, providing seamless north-south freight movement.

## Primary North-South Backbone

**Single-Line Canada-Mexico Corridor:**
Vancouver, BC → Calgary, AB → Winnipeg, MB → Minneapolis, MN → Kansas City, MO → Laredo, TX → Monterrey, Mexico → Mexico City / Lázaro Cárdenas

**Key Features:**
- Only single-line rail service connecting all three countries
- No interchange delays
- Seamless cross-border movements
- Fastest Canada-Mexico service

## Canadian Transcontinental Route

**Route:** Vancouver, BC → Calgary, AB → Regina, SK → Winnipeg, MB → Thunder Bay, ON → Toronto, ON → Montreal, QC → Saint John, NB

**Key Features:**
- Grain corridor (Prairies to ports)
- Potash export route
- Transcontinental freight
- Forest products

## Meridian Speedway (Joint with NS)

**Route:** Meridian, MS → Shreveport, LA (320 miles)
**Partnership:** Joint venture with Norfolk Southern
**Key Features:**
- Direct Southeast-to-West Coast/Mexico route
- Connection to NS eastern network
- Intermodal corridor

## Mexico Network (ex-Kansas City Southern)

**Pacific Corridor:**
- Laredo → Monterrey → Guadalajara → Lázaro Cárdenas (Pacific port)

**Gulf Corridor:**
- Laredo → Monterrey → San Luis Potosí → Mexico City → Veracruz (Gulf port)

**Central Mexico:**
- Monterrey hub connecting to all major Mexican industrial centers
- Aguascalientes (automotive)
- Querétaro (automotive, industrial)

## U.S. Midwest Routes

**Chicago Gateway:**
- Multiple routes into Chicago
- Interchange with all Class I carriers

**Kansas City Hub:**
- Central U.S. gateway
- Connection point between Canadian and Mexican operations
- Major classification yard (Knoche Yard)

**Texas Corridor:**
- Dallas/Fort Worth → Houston
- Dallas/Fort Worth → Laredo gateway

## Western Canada Routes

**Calgary-Vancouver:**
- Via Kicking Horse Pass (Canadian Rockies)
- Grain, potash, intermodal, forest products

**Edmonton-Vancouver:**
- Via Yellowhead Pass
- Alternative Rockies crossing

**Prairie Grain Line:**
- Regina-Winnipeg corridor
- Grain origins to Thunder Bay

## Border Crossings

**U.S.-Canada:**
- Portal, ND / Estevan, SK
- Emerson, MB / Pembina, ND
- Detroit, MI / Windsor, ON (via connections)
- Multiple other crossings

**U.S.-Mexico:**
- Laredo, TX / Nuevo Laredo, Mexico (primary gateway)
- Eagle Pass, TX / Piedras Negras, Mexico
