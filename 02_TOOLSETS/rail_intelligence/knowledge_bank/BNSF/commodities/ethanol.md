## Overview

BNSF transports ethanol and other renewable fuels for power companies, refineries, and manufacturers. The company has decades of experience in safely and efficiently moving these specialized energy materials.

## Commodities

- Ethanol (fuel-grade)
- Renewable Fuels (biodiesel, renewable diesel)
- Biofuels feedstocks

## Production Regions

- Iowa (leading ethanol production state)
- Nebraska
- Illinois
- Minnesota
- South Dakota
- Indiana

## Destination Markets

- Power companies
- Refineries (for blending with gasoline)
- Manufacturers
- West Coast terminals (California LCFS compliance)
- Gulf Coast export terminals
- East Coast distribution terminals

## Equipment

- DOT-111 compliant tank cars
- Unit train service (100+ car trains)
- Modern tank car fleet with enhanced safety features

## Routes

- Midwest ethanol belt to West Coast (California)
- Midwest to Gulf Coast terminals
- Midwest to East Coast via interchange
- Northern Transcon for Pacific Northwest delivery
- Southern Transcon for California delivery

## Service Types

- Unit trains for high-volume producers
- Manifest service for smaller shipments
- Door-to-door transload services

## Safety & Environmental Focus

BNSF emphasizes safety, fuel efficiency, and environmental responsibility in renewable fuel transportation, supporting the clean energy transition.

## Network Access

BNSF's extensive Midwest network connects major ethanol production facilities to West Coast and Gulf Coast markets via the Northern and Southern Transcons.
