# BNSF — Automotive Products

**Source:** https://www.bnsf.com/ship-with-bnsf/automotive-products/index.page
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Overview

BNSF provides specialized automotive shipping solutions for manufacturers and distributors, emphasizing "personalized service, on-time deliveries, logistical support, and damage-free" transportation. The company claims "the fastest multilevel performance in the industry married with the lowest claims ratio."

## Service Categories

### Finished Vehicles

BNSF offers multilevel car transport for vehicles manufactured domestically, delivering to dealerships nationwide. The service focuses on:
- Fast, secure transportation
- Lowest damage claims ratio in the industry
- On-time delivery performance
- Personalized logistics support

### Vehicle Parts

BNSF serves automobile manufacturers with competitive parts distribution, supporting:
- Assembly plant supply chains
- Just-in-time (JIT) delivery requirements
- Aftermarket parts distribution
- Critical supply chain partnerships

## Equipment

- **Multilevel Autoracks:**
  - Enclosed tri-level cars (standard)
  - Bi-level cars for larger vehicles (trucks, SUVs)
  - Damage-prevention features

- **Boxcars:**
  - Auto parts in containers or palletized
  - Weather-protected shipments

## Key Routes

- **West Coast Assembly Plants to National Markets:**
  - California plants to Midwest/East (via interchange)
  - Southern California to Texas/Southwest

- **Mexico Gateway:**
  - Import vehicles from Mexico assembly plants
  - Parts movements to/from Mexico

- **Midwest Assembly Plants:**
  - Kansas City auto production
  - Chicago area parts distribution

## Service Attributes

- **On-Time Performance:** Industry-leading delivery reliability
- **Damage-Free Claims:** Lowest claims ratio for damage
- **Personalized Service:** Dedicated logistics support teams
- **Supply Chain Partnership:** Long-term relationships with OEMs

## Facilities

- Vehicle distribution centers (VDCs)
- Auto ramps for loading/unloading
- Parts consolidation centers
- Transload facilities for truck-to-rail conversion

## Markets Served

- Original Equipment Manufacturers (OEMs)
- Auto dealerships nationwide
- Parts distributors
- Aftermarket suppliers

## Competitive Position

BNSF positions itself as supporting "one of the most critical industries in North America" by providing reliable automotive logistics for manufacturers facing significant global competition.

## Customer Support

Customers can access tools for:
- Pricing and rate quotes
- Shipment tracking
- Facility information
- Account management through customer portal (customer2.bnsf.com)
