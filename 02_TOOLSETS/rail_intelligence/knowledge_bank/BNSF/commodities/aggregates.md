# BNSF — Aggregates (Sand, Gravel, Crushed Stone)

**Source:** https://www.bnsf.com/ship-with-bnsf/industrial-products/aggregates.html
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Service Overview

BNSF moves **"sand, gravel, crushed stone, recycled concrete and asphalt concrete"** for customers across designated routes, primarily in the Western United States with joint-line access to Eastern markets.

## Product Types Transported

- Sand (construction, industrial, frac sand)
- Gravel
- Crushed stone
- Recycled concrete
- Asphalt concrete
- Other aggregate materials

## Geographic Coverage

**Primary Service Area:**
Western United States with access to most major aggregates production and consumption regions.

**Extended Reach:**
Joint-line access enables service to Eastern Markets through interchange with partner carriers.

**Route Limitations:**
**"BNSF provides service for aggregates only on designated routes."** Customers must verify route availability before planning shipments.

## Operating Models

**Single Car Service:**
- Standard carload options with published transit times
- Accessible via BNSF carload schedules tool
- Suitable for moderate-volume shippers

**Unit Train Service:**
- Available for customers meeting volume requirements
- Participants must **own or lease** their equipment
- Compliance with Grain Unit Train Operating Guidelines required (note: likely applies to aggregates despite grain terminology)

## Equipment Types

BNSF offers multiple car types to accommodate different aggregate products:

| Equipment Type | Cubic Capacity | Best For |
|---|---|---|
| **Open Top Hoppers** | 3,420-4,000 cu ft | General aggregates, sand, gravel, crushed stone |
| **Covered Hoppers (Small)** | 2,700 cu ft | Protected aggregates, dry sand |
| **Covered Hoppers (Medium)** | 3,100 cu ft | Protected aggregates |
| **Covered Hoppers (Large)** | 5,470 cu ft | High-volume covered loads |
| **Steel Service Gondolas** | 52-66 ft (length) | Bulk aggregates, recycled materials |

## Support Resources

**Rate Tool:**
"Get a Freight Rate" portal provides pricing for aggregates under various price authorities.

**Service Verification:**
Customers can "check to see if BNSF serves your destination" using switching guides to confirm route availability.

**Alternative Options:**
- Transload services for non-rail-served locations
- Economic development support for facility siting and expansion

## Key Advantages

✓ **Volume Efficiency:** One aggregate railcar replaces ~3-4 truckloads
✓ **Network Coverage:** Western US production regions to consumption markets
✓ **Equipment Variety:** Five equipment types accommodate different aggregate products
✓ **Unit Train Option:** High-volume shippers can achieve economies of scale
✓ **Environmental:** Rail reduces highway congestion and emissions vs. truck transport
