## Overview

BNSF moves chemicals used in manufacturing, including dry and liquid formulations. The company also transports plastics, including dry resin and liquid polymer materials. BNSF emphasizes safety, fuel efficiency, and environmental responsibility in chemical transportation.

## Chemical Categories

- **Industrial Chemicals:**
  - Caustic soda (sodium hydroxide)
  - Sulfuric acid
  - Hydrochloric acid
  - Solvents
  - Industrial gases (liquid form)

- **Agricultural Chemicals:**
  - Herbicides
  - Pesticides
  - Crop protection chemicals

- **Plastics & Resins:**
  - Polyethylene (PE) resin pellets
  - Polypropylene (PP) resin
  - PVC (polyvinyl chloride)
  - Liquid polymers and feedstocks

## Source Regions

- **Gulf Coast** (primary petrochemical hub)
  - Texas (Houston Ship Channel, Beaumont, Port Arthur)
  - Louisiana (Lake Charles, Baton Rouge)

- **Other Production Centers:**
  - California (Central Valley)
  - Pacific Northwest
  - Midwest chemical plants

## Destination Markets

- Manufacturing facilities nationwide
- Agricultural distribution centers
- Plastics processing plants
- Export terminals (for overseas shipment)

## Equipment

- **Tank Cars:**
  - DOT-111 and DOT-117 compliant tank cars
  - Pressure tank cars for hazardous materials
  - Coil cars for certain chemicals
  - Food-grade tank cars (segregated fleet)

- **Covered Hoppers:**
  - Plastic resin pellets
  - Dry chemical powders

## Routes

- Gulf Coast to Midwest manufacturing
- Gulf Coast to West Coast (Southern Transcon)
- Pacific Northwest to California
- Bidirectional chemical/plastics movements

## Service Types

- Unit trains for high-volume chemical producers
- Manifest freight for smaller shipments
- Transload services for truck-to-rail conversion
- Door-to-door logistics solutions

## Safety & Compliance

BNSF maintains compliance with:
- DOT hazardous materials regulations
- AAR tank car standards
- Environmental protection requirements
- Safety training for hazmat handling

## Facilities

- Chemical transload facilities
- Tank car cleaning and maintenance facilities
- Secure storage for hazardous materials

## Customized Solutions

BNSF develops customized solutions for chemical shippers, considering:
- Product compatibility
- Tank car specifications
- Routing requirements
- Safety protocols
- Regulatory compliance
