# BNSF — Petroleum & Crude Oil

**Source:** https://www.bnsf.com/ship-with-bnsf/energy/index.page
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Overview

BNSF transports crude oil, LPG (liquefied petroleum gas), and refined petroleum products for power companies, refineries, and manufacturers. The company has decades of experience in safely and efficiently moving energy materials.

## Commodities

- **Crude Oil:**
  - Light sweet crude
  - Heavy crude
  - Tight oil (shale production)

- **LPG (Liquefied Petroleum Gas):**
  - Propane
  - Butane
  - Natural gas liquids (NGLs)

- **Refined Products:**
  - Gasoline
  - Diesel fuel
  - Jet fuel
  - Fuel oil
  - Asphalt

## Production Regions

- **Bakken Formation** (North Dakota/Montana)
  - Major crude oil production region
  - Light sweet crude
  - Unit train loading facilities

- **Permian Basin** (Texas/New Mexico)
  - Crude oil and NGL production
  - Connection to Gulf Coast refineries

- **Other Sources:**
  - California oil fields
  - Wyoming production
  - Colorado oil & gas

## Destination Markets

- **Refineries:**
  - West Coast (California, Washington)
  - Gulf Coast (Texas, Louisiana)
  - Midwest (via interchange)

- **Distribution Terminals:**
  - Product terminals nationwide
  - LPG distribution centers
  - Export facilities

## Unit Train Operations

- Crude oil unit trains (100+ tank cars)
- High-volume movements from Bakken to refineries
- Dedicated train service for major producers
- Enhanced safety protocols for crude oil

## Equipment

- **DOT-117 Tank Cars:**
  - Enhanced safety standards for crude oil
  - Thicker tank shells
  - Improved thermal protection
  - Full-height head shields

- **LPG Pressure Tank Cars:**
  - Specialized pressure vessels
  - DOT-112/114 specifications

- **Refined Product Tank Cars:**
  - General service tank cars
  - Food-grade segregated fleet (for certain products)

## Routes

- **Bakken to West Coast:**
  - North Dakota → Montana → Washington refineries

- **Bakken to Gulf Coast:**
  - North Dakota → Southern Transcon → Texas

- **Permian to Gulf Coast:**
  - New Mexico/Texas → Houston/Beaumont area

- **Intra-California:**
  - Central Valley production to Bay Area refineries

## Safety & Regulatory Compliance

BNSF emphasizes:
- DOT hazardous materials compliance
- Enhanced tank car standards (DOT-117)
- Route risk assessments
- Emergency response planning
- Positive Train Control (PTC) on key routes

## Service Types

- Unit train service for producers
- Manifest freight for smaller volumes
- Dedicated train schedules
- Transload options for truck-to-rail conversion

## Market Trends

- Declining crude-by-rail volumes due to pipeline expansion
- Shift to enhanced safety tank cars (DOT-117)
- LPG and NGL growth (export markets)
- Refined products steady demand
