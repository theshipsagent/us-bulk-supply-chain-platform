# BNSF — Steel Products

**Source:** https://www.bnsf.com/ship-with-bnsf/industrial-products/steel-products.html
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Service Overview

BNSF transports a comprehensive range of steel products including **"flat and rolled steel"** with "consistent, reliable and cost-effective service" across North America's most efficient rail network.

## Product Types

- Structural steel
- Steel pipe
- Steel plate
- Flat and rolled steel
- Coil steel (open and covered transport)
- Some lumber products (bundled with steel service)

## Geographic Coverage

**Western United States:**
BNSF provides direct access to major steel production and consumption areas throughout the Western US.

**Eastern Markets:**
Joint-line access to Eastern Markets enables broader distribution capabilities beyond BNSF's primary network, facilitating transcontinental steel movements.

## Equipment Options

BNSF deploys specialized railcars optimized for different steel product types:

| Equipment Type | Length (ft) | Capacity (lbs) | Use Case |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Steel Service Gondolas** | 52-66 | 184,700-214,000 | General steel products |
| **Open Coil Railcars** | 34-42 | up to 243,800 | Steel coils (dry conditions) |
| **Covered Coil Railcars** | 34-56 | Standard | Weather protection for coils |
| **Bulkhead Flatcars** | 56-70 | Standard | Structural steel, pipe |
| **Pipe Flatcars** | — | 89-100 tons | Large-diameter pipe |

## Service Flexibility

**Volume Options:**
- Single carload shipments
- Multi-car movements
- Unit train service (requires customer-owned/leased equipment)

**Service Requirements:**
- Compliance with BNSF Unit Train Operating Guidelines for multi-car shipments
- Access to designated routes only

## Support Resources

**Transit Time Planning:**
Review carload schedules to estimate delivery windows

**Route Verification:**
Use BNSF switching guides to confirm service availability to destination

**Facility Development:**
Economic Development team provides assistance for facility expansion or new steel distribution centers

## Key Advantages

✓ **Specialized Equipment:** Six different railcar types to match product requirements
✓ **Network Reach:** Direct Western US access + Eastern market connections
✓ **Scalability:** Flexible service from single cars to unit trains
✓ **Efficiency:** Rail consolidates truck volumes, reducing transportation costs
