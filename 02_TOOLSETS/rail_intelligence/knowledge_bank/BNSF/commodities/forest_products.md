# BNSF — Forest Products

**Source:** https://www.bnsf.com/ship-with-bnsf/industrial-products/index.page
**Fetched:** 2026-02-23

---

## Overview

BNSF transports lumber, panel products, and paper products for the construction and manufacturing industries. The company provides customized solutions for forest products shippers across its western network.

## Commodities

- **Lumber:**
  - Dimension lumber
  - Timbers
  - Treated lumber
  - Specialty woods

- **Panel Products:**
  - Plywood
  - OSB (oriented strand board)
  - Particleboard
  - MDF (medium-density fiberboard)

- **Paper Products:**
  - Kraft paper
  - Containerboard
  - Newsprint
  - Specialty papers

- **Wood Pulp:**
  - Market pulp
  - Bleached pulp
  - Unbleached pulp

## Production Regions

- **Pacific Northwest:**
  - Washington (primary lumber/panel production)
  - Oregon
  - Idaho
  - Montana

- **British Columbia, Canada:**
  - Interchange with Canadian carriers for BC lumber

- **Southern Forests:**
  - Limited coverage via interchange

## Destination Markets

- Construction markets nationwide
- Manufacturing facilities (furniture, cabinets)
- Distribution centers
- Home improvement retailers
- Export terminals (Pacific Rim markets)

## Equipment

- **Centerbeam Flatcars:**
  - Standard for lumber and panel products
  - 60-80 foot length
  - Load securing systems

- **Boxcars:**
  - Enclosed cars for paper products
  - Weather protection for specialty products

- **Flatcars:**
  - Heavy timbers
  - Specialty dimensional products

## Routes

- **Pacific Northwest to California:**
  - Washington/Oregon mills → California construction markets

- **Pacific Northwest to Midwest:**
  - Via Northern Transcon to Chicago and beyond

- **Pacific Northwest to Texas:**
  - Via Southern route through California

- **Intra-regional:**
  - Mill-to-distribution center movements

## Service Types

- Unit trains for high-volume mills
- Manifest freight for smaller shipments
- Reload centers for distribution
- Transload facilities for truck delivery

## Facilities

- Lumber reload centers
- Panel product distribution facilities
- Transload operations for construction materials
- Team tracks for local lumber yards

## Interchange Points

BNSF interchanges forest products with:
- Union Pacific (for eastern delivery)
- Canadian Pacific Kansas City (for midwest/Canada)
- Canadian National (for eastern Canada)

## Market Trends

- Housing starts drive lumber demand
- Panel products follow construction cycles
- Paper products declining (digital transition)
- Export demand to Asia-Pacific markets

## Environmental Focus

BNSF emphasizes fuel efficiency and environmental responsibility in forest products transportation, supporting sustainable forestry supply chains.
